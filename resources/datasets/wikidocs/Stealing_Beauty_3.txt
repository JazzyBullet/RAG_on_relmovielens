The movie titled 'Stealing Beauty' is directed by Bernardo Bertolucci and was released in 1996. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Liv Tyler,Sinéad Cusack,Jeremy Irons,Jason Flemyng,Joseph Fiennes. It has a runtime of 116 minutes and languages used including English,Français,Deutsch,Italiano,Español, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Lucy Harmon, an American teenager is arriving in the lush Tuscan countryside to be sculpted by a family friend who lives in a beautiful villa. Lucy visited there four years earlier and exchanged a kiss with an Italian boy with whom she hopes to become reacquainted.
