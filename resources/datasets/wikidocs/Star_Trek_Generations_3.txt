The movie titled 'Star Trek: Generations' is directed by David Carson and was released in 1994. The genre of this movie is Action|Adventure|Sci-Fi, with main cast including Patrick Stewart,Jonathan Frakes,Brent Spiner,LeVar Burton,Michael Dorn. It has a runtime of 117 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: Captain Jean-Luc Picard and the crew of the Enterprise-D find themselves at odds with the renegade scientist Soran who is destroying entire star systems. Only one man can help Picard stop Soran's scheme...and he's been dead for seventy-eight years.
