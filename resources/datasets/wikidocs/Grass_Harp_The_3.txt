The movie titled 'Grass Harp, The' is directed by Charles Matthau and was released in 1995. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Piper Laurie,Sissy Spacek,Walter Matthau,Edward Furlong,Nell Carter. It has a runtime of 107 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: Based on the novel by Truman Capote, this often-witty coming-of-age drama looks at a young man growing up with an unusual family in the Deep South in the 1940s. Becoming an orphan in 1935, Collin moves to his dad's cousins Verena and Dolly. Verena is a rich, bossy businesswoman. Dolly, Collin and the maid revolt, moving to a tree house.
