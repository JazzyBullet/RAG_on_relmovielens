The movie titled 'Dumb & Dumber' is directed by Peter Farrelly and was released in 1994.0. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Jim Carrey,Jeff Daniels,Lauren Holly,Teri Garr,Charles Rocket. It has a runtime of 107 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: Lloyd and Harry are two men whose stupidity is really indescribable. When Mary, a beautiful woman, loses an important suitcase with money before she leaves for Aspen, the two friends (who have found the suitcase) decide to return it to her. After some "adventures" they finally get to Aspen where, using the lost money they live it up and fight for Mary's heart.
