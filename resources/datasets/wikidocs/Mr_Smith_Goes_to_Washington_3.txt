The movie titled 'Mr. Smith Goes to Washington' is directed by Frank Capra and was released in 1939.0. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including James Stewart,Jean Arthur,Claude Rains,Edward Arnold,Guy Kibbee. It has a runtime of 129 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: Naive and idealistic Jefferson Smith, leader of the Boy Rangers, is appointed to the United States Senate by the puppet governor of his state. He soon discovers, upon going to Washington, many shortcomings of the political process as his earnest goal of a national boys' camp leads to a conflict with the state political boss.
