The movie titled 'Man Who Knew Too Little, The' is directed by Jon Amiel and was released in 1997. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Mystery, with main cast including Bill Murray,Peter Gallagher,Joanne Whalley,Alfred Molina,Richard Wilson.
