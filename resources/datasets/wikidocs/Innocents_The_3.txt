The movie titled 'Innocents, The' is directed by Jack Clayton and was released in 1961. The genre of this movie is Thriller, with main cast including Deborah Kerr,Peter Wyngarde,Megs Jenkins,Michael Redgrave,Martin Stephens. It has a runtime of 100 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: A young governess for two children becomes convinced that the house and grounds are haunted by ghosts and that the children are being possessed.
