The movie titled 'Kagemusha' is directed by Akira Kurosawa and was released in 1980.0. The genre of this movie is Drama|War, with main cast including Tatsuya Nakadai,Tsutomu Yamazaki,Kenichi Hagiwara,Jinpachi Nezu,Hideji Ōtaki. It has a runtime of 180 minutes and languages used including 日本語, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: Akira Kurosawa's lauded feudal epic presents the tale of a petty thief who is recruited to impersonate Shingen, an aging warlord, in order to avoid attacks by competing clans. When Shingen dies, his generals reluctantly agree to have the impostor take over as the powerful ruler. He soon begins to appreciate life as Shingen, but his commitment to the role is tested when he must lead his troops into battle against the forces of a rival warlord.
