The movie titled 'Cavalcade' is directed by Frank Lloyd and was released in 1933.0. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Diana Wynyard,Clive Brook,Una O'Connor,Herbert Mundin,Beryl Mercer. The plot summary is as follows: A cavalcade of English life from New Year's Eve 1899 until 1933 is seen through the eyes of well-to-do Londoners Jane and Robert Marryot.
