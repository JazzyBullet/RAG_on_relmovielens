The movie titled 'Analyze This' is directed by Harold Ramis and was released in 1999. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Robert De Niro,Billy Crystal,Lisa Kudrow,Chazz Palminteri,Kresh Novakovic. It has a runtime of 103 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Countless wiseguy films are spoofed in this film that centers on the neuroses and angst of a powerful Mafia racketeer who suffers from panic attacks. When Paul Vitti needs help dealing with his role in the "family," unlucky shrink Dr. Ben Sobel is given just days to resolve Vitti's emotional crisis and turn him into a happy, well-adjusted gangster.
