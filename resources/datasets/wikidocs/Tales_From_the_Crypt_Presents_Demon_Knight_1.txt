Tales from the Crypt Presents: Demon Knight is a 1995 American black horror comedy film directed by Ernest Dickerson, starring Billy Zane, William Sadler, Jada Pinkett, Brenda Bakke, C. C. H. Pounder, Dick Miller, and Thomas Haden Church.
Demon Knight is a feature-length film presented by the HBO series Tales from the Crypt, and features scenes with the Crypt Keeper (voiced by John Kassir, as in the series) at the film's beginning and ending.
The film was met with mostly negative reviews. It was followed by a second standalone Tales from the Crypt film, Bordello of Blood (1996).
