The movie titled 'Ulee's Gold' is directed by Victor Nunez and was released in 1997. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Peter Fonda,Patricia Richardson,Christine Dunford,Tom Wood,Jessica Biel. It has a runtime of 112 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Third-generation Florida beekeeper Ulee Jackson may have gotten out of Vietnam alive, but he left a part of himself behind. Now he methodically tends his bees, carefully provides for his two grandchildren and keeps his emotions at bay. But when a long-buried secret threatens Ulee's business and family, he is forced to break through his emotional walls and confront the terror of his wounded spirit.
