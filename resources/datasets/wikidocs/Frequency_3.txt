The movie titled 'Frequency' is directed by Gregory Hoblit and was released in 2000.0. The genre of this movie is Drama|Thriller, with main cast including Dennis Quaid,Jim Caviezel,Shawn Doyle,Elizabeth Mitchell,Andre Braugher. It has a runtime of 118 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: When a rare phenomenon gives police officer John Sullivan the chance to speak to his father, 30 years in the past, he takes the opportunity to prevent his dad's tragic death.
