The movie titled 'Mystery, Alaska' is directed by Jay Roach and was released in 1999. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Russell Crowe,Hank Azaria,Mary McCormack,Burt Reynolds,Lolita Davidovich. It has a runtime of 119 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: In Mystery, Alaska, life revolves around the legendary Saturday hockey game at the local pond. But everything changes when the hometown team unexpectedly gets booked in an exhibition match against the New York Rangers. When quirky small-towners, slick promoters and millionaire athletes come together.
