I Don't Want to Talk About It (Spanish: De eso no se habla, Italian: Di questo non si parla) is a 1993 Argentine-Italian drama film directed by María Luisa Bemberg, starring Luisina Brando and Marcello Mastroianni. This film was shot in the historic quarter of Colonia del Sacramento, Uruguay. The script was based on a short story by Julio Llinas.
