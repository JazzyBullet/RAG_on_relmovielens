The movie titled 'Almost Famous' is directed by Cameron Crowe and was released in 2000. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Drama, with main cast including Patrick Fugit,Kate Hudson,Billy Crudup,Jason Lee,Frances McDormand. It has a runtime of 124 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: In 1973, 15-year-old William Miller's unabashed love of music and aspiration to become a rock journalist lands him an assignment from Rolling Stone magazine to interview and tour with the up-and-coming band, Stillwater.
