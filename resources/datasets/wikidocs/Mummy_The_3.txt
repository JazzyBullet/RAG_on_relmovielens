The movie titled 'Mummy, The' is directed by Terence Fisher and was released in 1959. The genre of this movie is Horror, with main cast including Peter Cushing,Christopher Lee,Yvonne Furneaux,Eddie Byrne,Felix Aylmer. It has a runtime of 88 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: One by one the archaeologists who discover the 4,000-year-old tomb of Princess Ananka are brutally murdered. Kharis, high priest in Egypt 40 centuries ago, has been brought to life by the power of the ancient gods and his sole purpose is to destroy those responsible for the desecration of the sacred tomb. But Isobel, wife of one of the explorers, resembles the beautiful princess, forcing the speechless and tormented monster to defy commands and abduct Isobel to an unknown fate.
