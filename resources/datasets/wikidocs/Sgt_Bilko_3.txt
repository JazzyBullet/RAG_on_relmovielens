The movie titled 'Sgt. Bilko' is directed by Jonathan Lynn and was released in 1996. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Steve Martin,Dan Aykroyd,Phil Hartman,Glenne Headly,Daryl Mitchell. It has a runtime of 90 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: The US army is known for churning out lean mean fighting machines intent on protecting our great nation. Sergeant Ernie Bilko is the leader of a ragtag group of the sorriest soldiers ever to enlist in the armed forces.
