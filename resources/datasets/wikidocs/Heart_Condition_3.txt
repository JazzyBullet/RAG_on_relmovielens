The movie titled 'Heart Condition' is directed by James D. Parriott and was released in 1990.0. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Bob Hoskins,Denzel Washington,Chloe Webb,Roger E. Mosley,Ja'net DuBois. It has a runtime of 100 minutes and languages used including Afrikaans,English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Racist police officer Jack Moony has a vendetta against Napoleon Stone, a charismatic black lawyer who is sleeping with Jack's old flame Crystal Gerrity. Jack has a heart attack, but his life is saved when he receives Stone's heart, since Stone died mysteriously the same night as Jack was stricken. Stone is not completely gone, however, and as a ghost he is all too happy to give Jack advice on how he should do his job and live his life.
