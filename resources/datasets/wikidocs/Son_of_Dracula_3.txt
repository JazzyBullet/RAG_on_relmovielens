The movie titled 'Son of Dracula' is directed by Robert Siodmak and was released in 1943. The genre of this movie is Horror, with main cast including Lon Chaney Jr.,Robert Paige,Louise Allbritton,Frank Craven,J. Edward Bromberg. It has a runtime of 80 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: Carpathian Count Alucard is invited to the U.S. by a young heiress. Her boyfriend and local officials are suspicious of the newcomer, who is interested in the "virile" soil of the new world.
