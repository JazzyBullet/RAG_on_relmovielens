The movie titled 'Meet John Doe' is directed by Frank Capra and was released in 1941.0. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Gary Cooper,Barbara Stanwyck,Edward Arnold,Walter Brennan,Spring Byington. It has a runtime of 122 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: As a parting shot, fired reporter Ann Mitchell prints a fake letter from unemployed "John Doe," who threatens suicide in protest of social ills. The paper is forced to rehire Ann and hires John Willoughby to impersonate "Doe." Ann and her bosses cynically milk the story for all it's worth, until the made-up "John Doe" philosophy starts a whole political movement.
