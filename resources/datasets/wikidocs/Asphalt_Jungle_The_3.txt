The movie titled 'Asphalt Jungle, The' is directed by John Huston and was released in 1950. The genre of this movie is Crime|Film-Noir, with main cast including Sterling Hayden,Louis Calhern,Sam Jaffe,Jean Hagen,James Whitmore. It has a runtime of 112 minutes and languages used including English,Deutsch, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: Recently paroled from prison, legendary burglar "Doc" Riedenschneider, with funding from Alonzo Emmerich, a crooked lawyer, gathers a small group of veteran criminals together in the Midwest for a big jewel heist.
