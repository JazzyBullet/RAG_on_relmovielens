The movie titled 'Specials, The' is directed by Craig Mazin and was released in 2000. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Rob Lowe,Jamie Kennedy,Thomas Haden Church,Jordan Ladd,Mike Schwartz. It has a runtime of 82 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: America's 7th Best Superhero Team, the Specials, are a group of geeks and oddballs. We get to see one day in their lives as fan and new member Nightbird joins the group, just in time for the group to get a new line of action figures. But the members' extreme personalities and personal issues threaten to rip the group apart.
