The genre of this movie is Horror|Thriller, with main cast including David Andrews,Kelly Wolf,Stephen Macht,Andrew Divoff,Vic Polizos. It has a runtime of 89 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: John Hall is a drifter who wanders into a small town in Maine. He needs a job and decides to seek employment at the community's top business: a large textile mill. He is hired to work the "graveyard shift" -- from around midnight to dawn -- and, along with a few others, he is charged with cleaning out the basement. This task strikes the workers as simple enough, but then, as they proceed deeper underground, they encounter an unspeakable monstrosity intent on devouring them all.
