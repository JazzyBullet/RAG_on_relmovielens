The movie titled 'Small Faces' is directed by Gillies MacKinnon and was released in 1996.0. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Iain Robertson,Joseph McFadden,Steven Duffy,Laura Fraser,Garry Sweeney. It has a runtime of 108 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Three teenage brothers, gang-member Bobby, troubled mama's boy Alan and self-assured prankster Lex, reside in a downtrodden section of Glasgow, Scotland, circa 1968. But while Bobby and Alan are beginning to experience the power of raging hormones, the story focuses on Lex, who begins a downward spiral after he accidentally shoots the leader of Bobby's gang. Lex's cockiness and immaturity unfortunately prevent him from understanding the effect his subsequent crimes will have on both himself, and on those around him.
