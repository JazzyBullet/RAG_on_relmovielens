The movie titled 'Godzilla 2000 (Gojira ni-sen mireniamu)' is directed by Takao Okawara and was released in 1999.0. The genre of this movie is Action|Adventure|Sci-Fi, with main cast including Takehiro Murata,Naomi Nishida,Mayu Suzuki,Hiroshi Abe,Shirō Sano. It has a runtime of 107 minutes and languages used including 日本語, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: An independent group of researchers called the Godzilla Prediction Network (GPN) actively track Godzilla as he makes landfall in Nemuro.  Matters are further complicated when a giant meteor is discovered in the Ibaragi Prefecture. The mysterious rock begins to levitate as it's true intentions for the world and Godzilla are revealed.
