The movie titled 'Not One Less (Yi ge dou bu neng shao)' is directed by Zhang Yimou and was released in 1999. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Wei Minzhi,Zhang Huike,Tian Zhenda,Gao Enman,Sun Zhimei. It has a runtime of 106 minutes and languages used including 普通话, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: Set in the People's Republic of China during the 1990s, the film centers on a 13-year-old substitute teacher, Wei Minzhi, in the Chinese countryside. Called in to substitute for a village teacher for one month, Wei is told not to lose any students.
