The movie titled 'Funhouse, The' is directed by Tobe Hooper and was released in 1981. The genre of this movie is Horror, with main cast including Elizabeth Berridge,Cooper Huckabee,Kevin Conway,Largo Woodruff,Miles Chapin. It has a runtime of 96 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Rebellious teen Amy defies her parents by going to a trashy carnival that has pulled into town. In tow are her boyfriend, Buzz, and their friends Liz  and Richie. Thinking it would be fun to spend the night in the campy "Funhouse" horror ride, the teens witness a murder by a deformed worker wearing a mask. Locked in, Amy and her friends must evade the murderous carnival workers and escape before it leaves town the next day.
