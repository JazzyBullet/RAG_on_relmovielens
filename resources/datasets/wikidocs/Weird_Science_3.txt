The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Anthony Michael Hall,Kelly LeBrock,Ilan Mitchell-Smith,Bill Paxton,Suzanne Snyder. The plot summary is as follows: Two unpopular teenagers, Gary and Wyatt, fail at all attempts to be accepted by their peers.
