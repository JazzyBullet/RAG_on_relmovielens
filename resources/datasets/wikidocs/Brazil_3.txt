The movie titled 'Brazil' is directed by Terry Gilliam and was released in 1985. The genre of this movie is Sci-Fi, with main cast including Jonathan Pryce,Kim Greist,Robert De Niro,Ian Holm,Bob Hoskins. It has a runtime of 143 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Low-level bureaucrat Sam Lowry escapes the monotony of his day-to-day life through a recurring daydream of himself as a virtuous hero saving a beautiful damsel. Investigating a case that led to the wrongful arrest and eventual death of an innocent man instead of wanted terrorist Harry Tuttle, he meets the woman from his daydream, and in trying to help her gets caught in a web of mistaken identities, mindless bureaucracy and lies.
