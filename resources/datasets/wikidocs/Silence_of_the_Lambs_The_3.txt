The movie titled 'Silence of the Lambs, The' is directed by Jonathan Demme and was released in 1991.0. The genre of this movie is Drama|Thriller, with main cast including Jodie Foster,Anthony Hopkins,Scott Glenn,Ted Levine,Anthony Heald. It has a runtime of 119 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Clarice Starling is a top student at the FBI's training academy.  Jack Crawford wants Clarice to interview Dr. Hannibal Lecter, a brilliant psychiatrist who is also a violent psychopath, serving life behind bars for various acts of murder and cannibalism.  Crawford believes that Lecter may have insight into a case and that Starling, as an attractive young woman, may be just the bait to draw him out.
