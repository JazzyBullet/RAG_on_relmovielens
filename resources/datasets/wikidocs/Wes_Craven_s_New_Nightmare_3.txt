The movie titled 'Wes Craven's New Nightmare' is directed by Wes Craven and was released in 1994. The genre of this movie is Horror, with main cast including Heather Langenkamp,Robert Englund,Miko Hughes,Wes Craven,David Newsom. It has a runtime of 112 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: A demonic force has chosen Freddy Krueger as its portal to the real world. Can Heather Langenkamp play the part of Nancy one last time and trap the evil trying to enter our world?
