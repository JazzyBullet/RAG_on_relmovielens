Prince of Central Park is a 2000 movie. The cast included Kathleen Turner, Danny Aiello, Harvey Keitel, and Cathy Moriarty. It was written and directed by John Leekley, and produced by Julius R. Nasso, Steven Seagal, and John P. Gulino. The film is a remake of the 1977 TV movie The Prince of Central Park, starring Ruth Gordon and T.J. Hargrave; both films were based on the novel The Prince of Central Park by Evan Rhodes.
