The movie titled 'Princess Mononoke, The (Mononoke Hime)' is directed by Hayao Miyazaki and was released in 1997. The genre of this movie is Action|Adventure|Animation, with main cast including Youji Matsuda,Yuriko Ishida,Yûko Tanaka,Kaoru Kobayashi,Masahiko Nishimura. It has a runtime of 134 minutes and languages used including 日本語, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: Ashitaka, a prince of the disappearing Emishi people, is cursed by a demonized boar god and must journey to the west to find a cure. Along the way, he encounters San, a young human woman fighting to protect the forest, and Lady Eboshi, who is trying to destroy it. Ashitaka must find a way to bring balance to this conflict.
