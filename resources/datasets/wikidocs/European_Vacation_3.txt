The movie titled 'European Vacation' is directed by Amy Heckerling and was released in 1985. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Chevy Chase,Beverly D'Angelo,Dana Hill,Jason Lively,Eric Idle. It has a runtime of 95 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: The Griswalds win a vacation to Europe on a game show, and so pack their bags for the continent. They do their best to catch the flavor of Europe, but they just don't know how to be be good tourists. Besides, they have trouble taking holidays in countries where they CAN speak the language.
