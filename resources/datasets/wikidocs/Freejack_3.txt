The movie titled 'Freejack' is directed by Geoff Murphy and was released in 1992. The genre of this movie is Action|Sci-Fi, with main cast including Emilio Estevez,Mick Jagger,Rene Russo,Anthony Hopkins,Jonathan Banks. It has a runtime of 110 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Time-traveling bounty hunters find a doomed race-car driver in the past and bring him to 2009 New York, where his mind will be replaced with that of a terminally ill billionaire.
