The movie titled 'Big Bully' is directed by Steve Miner and was released in 1996. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Drama, with main cast including Rick Moranis,Tom Arnold,Julianne Phillips,Carol Kane,Jeffrey Tambor. It has a runtime of 90 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: A writer returns to his hometown where he faces the childhood nemesis whose life he ultimately ruined, only the bully wants to relive their painful past by torturing him once again.
