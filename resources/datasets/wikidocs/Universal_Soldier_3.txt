The movie titled 'Universal Soldier' is directed by Roland Emmerich and was released in 1992.0. The genre of this movie is Action|Sci-Fi, with main cast including Jean-Claude Van Damme,Dolph Lundgren,Ally Walker,Ed O'Ross,Ralf Moeller. It has a runtime of 102 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: An American soldier who had been killed during the Vietnam War is revived 25 years later by the military as a semi-android, UniSols, a high-tech soldier of the future. After the failure of the initiative to erase all the soldier's memories, he begins to experience flashbacks that are forcing him to recall his past.
