The movie titled 'Working Girl' is directed by Mike Nichols and was released in 1988. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Drama, with main cast including Melanie Griffith,Harrison Ford,Sigourney Weaver,Alec Baldwin,Joan Cusack. It has a runtime of 114 minutes and languages used including Deutsch,English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Tess McGill is an ambitious secretary with a unique approach for climbing the ladder to success. When her classy, but villainous boss breaks a leg skiing, Tess takes over her office, her apartment and even her wardrobe. She creates a deal with a handsome investment banker that will either take her to the top, or finish her off for good.
