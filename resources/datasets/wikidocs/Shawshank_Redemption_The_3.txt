The movie titled 'Shawshank Redemption, The' is directed by Frank Darabont and was released in 1994. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Tim Robbins,Morgan Freeman,Bob Gunton,William Sadler,Clancy Brown. It has a runtime of 142 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Framed in the 1940s for the double murder of his wife and her lover, upstanding banker Andy Dufresne begins a new life at the Shawshank prison, where he puts his accounting skills to work for an amoral warden. During his long stretch in prison, Dufresne comes to be admired by the other inmates -- including an older prisoner named Red -- for his integrity and unquenchable sense of hope.
