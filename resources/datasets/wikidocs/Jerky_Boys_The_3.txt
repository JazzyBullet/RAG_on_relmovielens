The movie titled 'Jerky Boys, The' is directed by James Melkonian and was released in 1995. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including John G. Brennan,Kamal Ahmed,Alan Arkin,William Hickey,Vincent Pastore. It has a runtime of 82 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: When two unemployed telephone pranksters decide to use their vocal "talents" to impersonate a Chicago mob boss and curry favor with organized crime in New York, the trouble begins. It isn't long before Johnny and Kamal (the "Jerky Boys" of crank call fame) are wanted by the local mafia, the police, and their neighbor.
