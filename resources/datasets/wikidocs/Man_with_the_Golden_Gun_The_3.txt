The movie titled 'Man with the Golden Gun, The' is directed by Guy Hamilton and was released in 1974. The genre of this movie is Action, with main cast including Roger Moore,Christopher Lee,Britt Ekland,Maud Adams,Hervé Villechaize. It has a runtime of 125 minutes and languages used including ภาษาไทย,广州话 / 廣州話,English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: Cool government operative James Bond searches for a stolen invention that can turn the sun's heat into a destructive weapon. He soon crosses paths with the menacing Francisco Scaramanga, a hitman so skilled he has a seven-figure working fee. Bond then joins forces with the swimsuit-clad Mary Goodnight, and together they track Scaramanga to a Thai tropical isle hideout where the killer-for-hire lures the slick spy into a deadly maze for a final duel.
