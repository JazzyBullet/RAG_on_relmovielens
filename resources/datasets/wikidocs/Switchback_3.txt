The movie titled 'Switchback' is directed by Jeb Stuart and was released in 1997. The genre of this movie is Thriller, with main cast including Dennis Quaid,Danny Glover,R. Lee Ermey,Jared Leto,Ted Levine. It has a runtime of 118 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: After FBI agent Frank Lacrosse believes his son was kidnapped by a notorious serial killer, he travels to Amarillo, Texas, where he believes the murderer is in hiding. Although officially taken off the case because of its personal significance to him, Frank continues to pursue the killer, causing concern for local sheriff Buck Olmstead. When another victim pops up at a nearby car garage, Frank knows that he is as close as he'll ever be to tracking down the elusive killer.
