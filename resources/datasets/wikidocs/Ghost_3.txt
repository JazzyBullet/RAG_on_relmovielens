The movie titled 'Ghost' is directed by Jerry Zucker and was released in 1990.0. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Romance|Thriller, with main cast including Patrick Swayze,Demi Moore,Whoopi Goldberg,Tony Goldwyn,Vincent Schiavelli. It has a runtime of 127 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: Sam Wheat is a banker, Molly Jensen is an artist, and the two are madly in love. However, when Sam is murdered by his friend and corrupt business partner Carl Bruner over a shady business deal, he is left to roam the earth as a powerless spirit. When he learns of Carl's betrayal, Sam must seek the help of psychic Oda Mae Brown to set things right and protect Molly from Carl and his goons.
