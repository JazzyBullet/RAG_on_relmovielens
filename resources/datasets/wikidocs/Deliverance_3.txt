The movie titled 'Deliverance' is directed by John Boorman and was released in 1972. The genre of this movie is Adventure|Thriller, with main cast including Jon Voight,Burt Reynolds,Ned Beatty,Ronny Cox,Ed Ramey. It has a runtime of 109 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Intent on seeing the Cahulawassee River before it's turned into one huge lake, outdoor fanatic Lewis Medlock takes his friends on a river-rafting trip they'll never forget into the dangerous American back-country.
