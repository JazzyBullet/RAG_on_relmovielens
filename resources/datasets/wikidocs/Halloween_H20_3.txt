The movie titled 'Halloween: H20' is directed by Steve Miner and was released in 1998. The genre of this movie is Horror|Thriller, with main cast including Jamie Lee Curtis,Josh Hartnett,Adam Arkin,Michelle Williams,Adam Hann-Byrd. It has a runtime of 86 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Two decades after surviving a massacre on October 31, 1978, former baby sitter Laurie Strode finds herself hunted by persistent knife-wielder Michael Myers. Laurie now lives in Northern California under an assumed name, where she works as the headmistress of a private school. But it's not far enough to escape Myers, who soon discovers her whereabouts. As Halloween descends upon Laurie's peaceful community, a feeling of dread weighs upon her -- with good reason.
