The movie titled 'Celebrity' is directed by Woody Allen and was released in 1998.0. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Kenneth Branagh,Judy Davis,Joe Mantegna,Leonardo DiCaprio,Charlize Theron. It has a runtime of 113 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: The career and personal life of writer Lee are at a standstill, so he divorces his bashful wife, Robin, and dives into a new job as an entertainment journalist. His assignments take him to the swankiest corners of Manhattan, but as he jumps from one lavish party to another and engages in numerous empty romances, he starts to doubt the worth of his work. Meanwhile, top TV producer Tony falls for Robin and introduces her to the world of celebrity.
