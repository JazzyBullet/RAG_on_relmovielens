The movie titled 'Cosi' is directed by Mark Joffe and was released in 1996.0. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Ben Mendelsohn,Barry Otto,Toni Collette,Rachel Griffiths,Aden Young. It has a runtime of 102 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Lewis, a young amateur theater director, is offered a job with a governmental program for the rehabilitation of mentally ill patients in a Sydney institution. His project is overrun by one of the patients who wants to stage the opera Cosi Fan Tutte by Mozart despite the fact that none of the patients are able to sing and none of them speak Italian. A comedy of errors ensues, but one which unifies the patients and their director in unexpected ways.
