The movie titled 'Of Mice and Men' is directed by Gary Sinise and was released in 1992. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including John Malkovich,Gary Sinise,Ray Walston,Casey Siemaszko,Sherilyn Fenn. It has a runtime of 110 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: Two drifters, one a gentle but slow giant, try to make money working the fields during the Depression so they can fulfill their dreams.
