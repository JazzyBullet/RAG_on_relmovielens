The movie titled 'Attack of the Killer Tomatoes!' is directed by John De Bello and was released in 1978. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Horror, with main cast including David Miller,George Wilson,Sharon Taylor,J. Stephen Peace,Ernie Meyers. It has a runtime of 87 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: After a wave of reports of mysterious attacks involving people and pets being eaten by the traditionally docile fruit, a special government task force is set up to investigate the violent fruit and put a stop to their murderous spree.
