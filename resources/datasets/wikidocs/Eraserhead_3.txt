The movie titled 'Eraserhead' is directed by David Lynch and was released in 1977. The genre of this movie is Drama|Horror, with main cast including Jack Nance,Charlotte Stewart,Allen Joseph,Jeanne Bates,Judith Roberts. It has a runtime of 89 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: First time father Henry Spencer tries to survive his industrial environment, his angry girlfriend, and the unbearable screams of his newly born mutant child. David Lynch arrived on the scene in 1977, almost like a mystical UFO gracing the landscape of LA with its enigmatic radiance. His inaugural work, "Eraserhead" (1977), stood out as a cinematic anomaly, painting a surreal narrative of a young man navigating a dystopian, industrialized America, grappling not only with his tumultuous home life but also contending with an irate girlfriend and a mutant child.
