The movie titled 'Barcelona' is directed by Whit Stillman and was released in 1994. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Romance, with main cast including Taylor Nichols,Chris Eigeman,Tushka Bergen,Mira Sorvino,Pep Munné. It has a runtime of 100 minutes and languages used including Español,English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: During the 1980s, uptight Ted Boynton is a salesman working in the Barcelona office of a Chicago-based company. He receives an unexpected visit from his cousin Fred, a naval officer who has come to Spain on a public relations mission for a U.S. fleet. Not exactly friends in the past, Ted and Fred strike up relationships with women in the Spanish city and experience conflicts -- Ted with his employer, and Fred with the Barcelona community.
