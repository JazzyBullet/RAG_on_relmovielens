The movie titled 'Nurse Betty' is directed by Neil LaBute and was released in 2000. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Thriller, with main cast including Morgan Freeman,Renée Zellweger,Chris Rock,Greg Kinnear,Aaron Eckhart. It has a runtime of 108 minutes and languages used including Español,English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: What happens when a person decides that life is merely a state of mind? If you're Betty, a small-town waitress and soap opera fan from Fair Oaks, Kansas, you refuse to believe that you can't be with the love of your life just because he doesn't really exist. After all, life is no excuse for not living. Traumatized by a savage event, Betty enters into a fugue state that allows -- even encourages -- her to keep functioning... in a kind of alternate reality.
