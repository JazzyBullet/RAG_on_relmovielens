The movie titled 'Frighteners, The' is directed by Peter Jackson and was released in 1996. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Horror, with main cast including Michael J. Fox,Trini Alvarado,Peter Dobson,John Astin,Jeffrey Combs. It has a runtime of 110 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Once an architect, Frank Bannister now passes himself off as an exorcist of evil spirits. To bolster his facade, he claims his "special" gift is the result of a car accident that killed his wife. But what he does not count on is more people dying in the small town where he lives. As he tries to piece together the supernatural mystery of these killings, he falls in love with the wife of one of the victims and deals with a crazy FBI agent.
