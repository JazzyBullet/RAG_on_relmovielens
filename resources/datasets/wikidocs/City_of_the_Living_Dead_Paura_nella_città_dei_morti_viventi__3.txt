The movie titled 'City of the Living Dead (Paura nella città dei morti viventi)' is directed by Lucio Fulci and was released in 1980. The genre of this movie is Horror, with main cast including Christopher George,Catriona MacColl,Carlo De Mejo,Giovanni Lombardo Radice,Janet Ågren. It has a runtime of 93 minutes and languages used including Italiano, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: A woman seemingly dies of fright after participating in a séance where she sees a vision of a Dunwich priest hanging himself in a church cemetery. New York City reporter Peter Bell investigates and learns that the priest's suicide has somehow opened a portal to Hell and must be sealed by All Saints Day, or else the dead will overtake humanity.
