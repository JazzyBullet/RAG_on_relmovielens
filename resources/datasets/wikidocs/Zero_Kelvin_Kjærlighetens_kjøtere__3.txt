The movie titled 'Zero Kelvin (Kjærlighetens kjøtere)' is directed by Hans Petter Moland and was released in 1995.0. The genre of this movie is Action, with main cast including Stellan Skarsgård,Gard B. Eidsvold,Bjørn Sundquist,Camilla Martens,Paul-Ottar Haga. It has a runtime of 118 minutes and languages used including Norsk, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: Larsen, an aspiring poet in '20s Oslo, leaves his girlfriend to spend a year as a trapper in East Greenland. There he is teamed with a seemingly rough old sailor/trapper, Randbæk, and a scientist, Holm. Trapped in a tiny hut together as the Arctic winter sets in, a complex and intense love/hate relationship develops between Randbæk and Larsen, who are more similar than either would like to admit. A powerful psychological and physical drama set against stunningly bleak Arctic scenery.
