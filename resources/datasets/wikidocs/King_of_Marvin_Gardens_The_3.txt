The movie titled 'King of Marvin Gardens, The' is directed by Bob Rafelson and was released in 1972. The genre of this movie is Crime|Drama, with main cast including Jack Nicholson,Bruce Dern,Ellen Burstyn,Julia Anne Robinson,Scatman Crothers. It has a runtime of 103 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Jason Staebler lives on the Boardwalk and fronts for the local mob in Atlantic City. He is a dreamer who asks his brother David, a radio personality from Philadelphia, to help him build a paradise on a Pacific Island, which might be just another of his pie-in-the-sky schemes. Inevitably, complications begin to pile up.
