The movie titled 'Perfect Storm, The' is directed by Wolfgang Petersen and was released in 2000. The genre of this movie is Action|Adventure|Thriller, with main cast including George Clooney,Mark Wahlberg,Diane Lane,John C. Reilly,William Fichtner. It has a runtime of 130 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: In October 1991, a confluence of weather conditions combined to form a killer storm in the North Atlantic. Caught in the storm was the sword-fishing boat Andrea Gail.
