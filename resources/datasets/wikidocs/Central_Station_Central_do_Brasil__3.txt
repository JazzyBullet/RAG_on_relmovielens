The movie titled 'Central Station (Central do Brasil)' is directed by Walter Salles and was released in 1998. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Fernanda Montenegro,Vinícius de Oliveira,Marília Pêra,Othon Bastos,Otávio Augusto. It has a runtime of 110 minutes and languages used including Português, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: An emotive journey of a former school teacher, who writes letters for illiterate people, and a young boy, whose mother has just died, as they search for the father he never knew.
