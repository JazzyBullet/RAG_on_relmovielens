The movie titled 'Phantasm III: Lord of the Dead' is directed by Don Coscarelli and was released in 1994. The genre of this movie is Horror, with main cast including A. Michael Baldwin,Reggie Bannister,Bill Thornbury,Gloria Lynne Henry,Kevin Connors. It has a runtime of 91 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: The Tall Man, that imposing menace from Morningside Mortuary, is back and once again haunting the thoughts of the now-adult Mike and his friend, ex-Ice Cream vendor Reggie. The two continue their hunt for the mysterious figure and in his path of destruction encounter a variety of dangerous situations, friends and enemies.
