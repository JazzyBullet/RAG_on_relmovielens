The movie titled 'Jerry & Tom' is directed by Saul Rubinek and was released in 1998. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Joe Mantegna,Sam Rockwell,Maury Chaykin,Ted Danson,Charles Durning. It has a runtime of 107 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: Tom and Jerry are two hit men, they work by day at a third-rate second-hand car dealership. Tom is a veteran and Jerry is a novice in their business, and their attitude toward their profession differs a lot. It shows when Tom is required to kill his old friend Karl.
