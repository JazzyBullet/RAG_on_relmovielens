The movie titled 'Artemisia' is directed by Agnès Merlet and was released in 1997. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Valentina Cervi,Michel Serrault,Miki Manojlović,Luca Zingaretti,Emmanuelle Devos. It has a runtime of 98 minutes and languages used including Français, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: The story of Artemisia Gentileschi (1593-1653), one of the first well-known female painters, including her youth, when she was guided and protected by her father, the painter Orazio Gentileschi.
