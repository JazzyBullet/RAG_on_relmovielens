The movie titled 'I'll Be Home For Christmas' is directed by Arlene Sanford and was released in 1998. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Romance, with main cast including Jonathan Taylor Thomas,Jessica Biel,Adam LaVorgna,Gary Cole,Eve Gordon. It has a runtime of 86 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: Estranged from his father, college student Jake is lured home to New York for Christmas with the promise of receiving a classic Porsche as a gift. When the bullying football team dumps him in the desert in a Santa suit, Jake is left without identification or money to help him make the journey. Meanwhile, his girlfriend, Allie, does not know where he is, and accepts a cross-country ride from Jake's rival, Eddie.
