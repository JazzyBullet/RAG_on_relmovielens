The movie titled 'Happiest Millionaire, The' is directed by Norman Tokar and was released in 1967. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Musical, with main cast including Fred MacMurray,Greer Garson,Gladys Cooper,Geraldine Page,Tommy Steele. It has a runtime of 169 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: A happy and unbelievably lucky young Irish immigrant, John Lawless, lands a job as the butler of an unconventional millionaire, Biddle. His daughter, Cordelia Drexel Biddle, tires of the unusual antics of her father--especially since the nice young men around town all fear him. Wouldn't you fear a father-in-law that keeps alligators for pets and teaches boxing at his daily Bible classes?
