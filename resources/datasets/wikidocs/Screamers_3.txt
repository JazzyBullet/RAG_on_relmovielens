The movie titled 'Screamers' is directed by Christian Duguay and was released in 1995. The genre of this movie is Sci-Fi|Thriller, with main cast including Peter Weller,Jennifer Rubin,Roy Dupuis,Andrew Lauer,Liliana Głąbczyńska. It has a runtime of 108 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: SIRIUS 6B, Year 2078. On a distant mining planet ravaged by a decade of war, scientists have created the perfect weapon: a blade-wielding, self-replicating race of killing devices known as Screamers designed for one purpose only -- to hunt down and destroy all enemy life forms.
