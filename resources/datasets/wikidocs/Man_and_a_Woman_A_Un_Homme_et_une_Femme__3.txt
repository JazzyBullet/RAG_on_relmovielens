The movie titled 'Man and a Woman, A (Un Homme et une Femme)' is directed by Claude Lelouch and was released in 1966. The genre of this movie is Drama|Romance, with main cast including Anouk Aimée,Jean-Louis Trintignant,Pierre Barouh,Valérie Lagrange,Antoine Sire. It has a runtime of 102 minutes and languages used including Français, with a Certificate rating of nan.
