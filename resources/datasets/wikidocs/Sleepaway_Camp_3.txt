The movie titled 'Sleepaway Camp' is directed by Robert Hiltzik and was released in 1983. The genre of this movie is Horror, with main cast including Felissa Rose,Jonathan Tiersten,Karen Fields,Christopher Collet,Mike Kellin. It has a runtime of 85 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. Not long after Angela's arrival, things start to go horribly wrong for anyone with sinister or less than honorable intentions.
