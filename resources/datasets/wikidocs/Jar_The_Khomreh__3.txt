The movie titled 'Jar, The (Khomreh)' is directed by Ebrahim Forouzesh and was released in 1994. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Behzad Khodaveisi,Fatemeh Azrah,Abbas Khavaninzadeh,Hassein Balai,Alireza Haji-Ghasemi. It has a runtime of 86 minutes and languages used including فارسی, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: In the yard of an Iranian village school stands in the shade of a tree a large stoneware jar from which all the pupils drink fresh water. On an unfortunate day, the jar starts leaking. The schoolmaster tries hard to get a new one but in vain. The only solution is to have it fixed...
