The movie titled 'Croupier' is directed by Mike Hodges and was released in 1998. The genre of this movie is Crime|Drama, with main cast including Clive Owen,Kate Hardie,Alex Kingston,Gina McKee,Nicholas Ball. It has a runtime of 94 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Jack Manfred is an aspiring writer who to make ends meet, takes a job as a croupier. Jack remains an observer, knowing that everything in life is a gamble and that gamblers are born to lose. Inevitably, he gets sucked into the world of the casino which takes its toll on his relationships and the novel he is writing.
