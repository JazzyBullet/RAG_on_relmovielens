The movie titled 'Wooden Man's Bride, The (Wu Kui)' is directed by Huang Jianxin and was released in 1994. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Gu Bao-Ming,Mingjun Kao,Lan Wang,Wang Yumei,Wang Fuli. It has a runtime of 113 minutes and languages used including 普通话, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: After her groom is killed in a bandit ambush, a young peasant bride must move into the home of her wealthy mother-in-law, Madame Liu. In a perverse tribute to her fallen son, Madame Liu imposes on her new daughter-in-law a life of domestic servitude. In the midst of her misery, the young widow finds new romance with another peasant, the gentle and brave Kui. But when Madame Liu discovers her infidelity, she vows to keep the lovers apart.
