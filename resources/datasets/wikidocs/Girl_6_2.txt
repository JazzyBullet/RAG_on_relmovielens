Girl 6 is the soundtrack album to the Spike Lee film of the same name. All songs were written by Prince.  It was released on March 19, 1996, by Warner Bros. Records. The album consists of mostly previously released songs from Prince and related artists such as the Family, Vanity 6, and the New Power Generation.
