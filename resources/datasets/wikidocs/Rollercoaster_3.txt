The movie titled 'Rollercoaster' is directed by James Goldstone and was released in 1977.0. The genre of this movie is Drama|Thriller, with main cast including George Segal,Richard Widmark,Timothy Bottoms,Harry Guardino,Susan Strasberg. It has a runtime of 119 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: A young terrorist kills and injures patrons of a Norfolk amusement park by placing homemade explosives on the track of one of its roller coasters.
