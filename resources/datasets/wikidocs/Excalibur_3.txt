The movie titled 'Excalibur' is directed by John Boorman and was released in 1981. The genre of this movie is Action|Drama|Fantasy|Romance, with main cast including Nigel Terry,Helen Mirren,Nicholas Clay,Cherie Lunghi,Paul Geoffrey. It has a runtime of 141 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: A surreal adaptation of Sir Thomas Malory's "Le Morte d'Arthur", chronicling Arthur Pendragon's conception, his rise to the throne, the search by his Knights of the Round Table for the Holy Grail, and ultimately his death.
