The movie titled 'Eraser' is directed by Chuck Russell and was released in 1996. The genre of this movie is Action|Thriller, with main cast including Arnold Schwarzenegger,Vanessa Williams,James Caan,James Coburn,Robert Pastorelli. It has a runtime of 115 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: U.S. Marshall John Kruger erases the identities of people enrolled in the Witness Protection Program. His current assignment is to protect Lee Cullen, who's uncovered evidence that the weapons manufacturer she works for has been selling to terrorist groups. When Kruger discovers that there's a corrupt agent within the program, he must guard his own life while trying to protect Lee's.
