The movie titled 'Cheetah' is directed by Jeff Blyth and was released in 1989. The genre of this movie is Adventure|Children's, with main cast including Keith Coogan,Lucy Deakins,Timothy Landfield,Colin Mothupi,Breon Gorman. It has a runtime of 83 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of G. The plot summary is as follows: An American boy and girl, spending six months in Kenya with their scientist parents adopt a cheetah, only to realize that they must set it loose so that it can learn to hunt and be free. However, when the animal is captured by poachers planning to race it against greyhounds, the two city kids, together with a young African goat herder they befriended, head off into the wild to rescue the cheetah.
