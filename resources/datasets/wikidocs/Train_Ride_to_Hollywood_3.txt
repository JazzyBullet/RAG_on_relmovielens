The movie titled 'Train Ride to Hollywood' is directed by Charles R. Rondeau and was released in 1975.0. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Willis Draffen Jr.,Charles Love,Charles McCormick,Harry Williams,Michael Payne. It has a runtime of 89 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of G. The plot summary is as follows: Harry Williams, member of the rhythm & blues band, Bloodstone, is about to go onstage for a concert when he is hit on the head. The rest that follows is his dream. The four band members become conductors on a train filled with characters and (impersonated) actors from the 1930s, such as W.C. Fields, Dracula, and Scarlett O'Hara. Various songs are featured. The singing conductors are obliged to solve a mystery; Marlon Brando is murdering Nelson Eddy, Jeanette McDonald and others by suffocating them in his armpits. A wacky funeral, a fight with a gorilla, and the threat of being turned into a wax museum figure are all part of Harry's dream.
