The movie titled 'Black Sabbath (Tre Volti Della Paura, I)' is directed by Mario Bava and was released in 1963. The genre of this movie is Horror, with main cast including Boris Karloff,Mark Damon,Michèle Mercier,Susy Andersen,Lidia Alfonsi. It has a runtime of 95 minutes and languages used including Italiano, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: Three short tales of supernatural horror. In “The Telephone,” a woman is plagued by threatening phone calls. In "The Wurdalak,” a family is preyed upon by vampiric monsters. In “The Drop of Water,” a deceased medium wreaks havoc on the living.
