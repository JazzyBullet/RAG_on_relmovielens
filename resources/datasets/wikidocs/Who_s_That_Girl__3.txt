The movie titled 'Who's That Girl?' is directed by James Foley and was released in 1987. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Madonna,Griffin Dunne,Bibi Besch,John McMartin,John Mills. It has a runtime of 92 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: An uptight New York tax lawyer gets his life turned upside down, all in a single day, when he's asked to escort a feisty and free-spirited female ex-convict whom asks him to help prove her innocence of her crime.
