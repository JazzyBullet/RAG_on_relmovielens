The movie titled 'Avalanche' is directed by Corey Allen and was released in 1978. The genre of this movie is Action, with main cast including Rock Hudson,Mia Farrow,Robert Forster,Jeanette Nolan,Rick Moses. It has a runtime of 94 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG.
