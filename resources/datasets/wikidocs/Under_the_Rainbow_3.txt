The movie titled 'Under the Rainbow' is directed by Steve Rash and was released in 1981. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Chevy Chase,Carrie Fisher,Eve Arden,Joseph Maher,Robert Donner. It has a runtime of 98 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: In World War II era Los Angeles, the manager of the Culver Hotel leaves his nephew in charge for a weekend. The nephew changes the name to the Hotel Rainbow and overbooks with royalty, assassins, secret agents, Japanese tourists, and munchkins. Secret Service agent Bruce Thorpe and casting director Annie Clark find romance amidst the intrigue and confusion.
