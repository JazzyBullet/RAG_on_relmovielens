The movie titled 'Ashes of Time' is directed by Wong Kar-wai and was released in 1994. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Leslie Cheung,Tony Leung Ka-fai,Brigitte Lin,Jacky Cheung,Tony Leung Chiu-wai. It has a runtime of 100 minutes and languages used including 广州话 / 廣州話, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Ouyang Feng is a heartbroken and cynical man who spends his days in the desert, connecting expert swordsmen with those seeking revenge and willing to pay for it. Throughout five seasons in exile, Ouyang spins tales of his clients' unrequited loves and unusual acts of bravery.
