The movie titled 'Ordinary People' is directed by Robert Redford and was released in 1980. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Timothy Hutton,Donald Sutherland,Mary Tyler Moore,Judd Hirsch,M. Emmet Walsh. It has a runtime of 124 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Beth, Calvin, and their son Conrad are living in the aftermath of the death of the other son. Conrad is overcome by grief and misplaced guilt to the extent of a suicide attempt. He is in therapy. Beth had always preferred his brother and is having difficulty being supportive to Conrad. Calvin is trapped between the two trying to hold the family together.
