The movie titled 'Phantasm II' is directed by Don Coscarelli and was released in 1988. The genre of this movie is Horror, with main cast including James Le Gros,Reggie Bannister,Angus Scrimm,Paula Irvine,Samantha Phillips. It has a runtime of 97 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Mike, after his release from a psychiatric hospital, teams up with his old pal Reggie to hunt down the Tall Man, who is at it again. A mysterious, beautiful girl has also become part of Mike's dreams, and they must find her before the Tall Man does.
