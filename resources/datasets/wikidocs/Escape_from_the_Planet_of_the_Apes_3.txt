The movie titled 'Escape from the Planet of the Apes' is directed by Don Taylor and was released in 1971. The genre of this movie is Action|Sci-Fi, with main cast including Roddy McDowall,Kim Hunter,Bradford Dillman,Natalie Trundy,Eric Braeden. It has a runtime of 98 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of G. The plot summary is as follows: The world is shocked by the appearance of three talking chimpanzees, who arrived mysteriously in a spacecraft. Intrigued by their intelligence, humans use them for research - until the apes attempt to escape.
