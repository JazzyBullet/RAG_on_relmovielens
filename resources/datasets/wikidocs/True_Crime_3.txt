The movie titled 'True Crime' is directed by Pat Verducci and was released in 1996.0. The genre of this movie is Mystery|Thriller, with main cast including Alicia Silverstone,Kevin Dillon,Bill Nunn,Michael Bowen,Ann Devaney. It has a runtime of 94 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Mary Giordano is a bright, intelligent student who goes to a catholic school. She also has an addiction to mystery novels and detective magazines, which inspire her to do her own detective work. When she starts snooping around on the case of a murderer of teenage girls, it gets her in hot water with her mentor Detective Jerry Gunn. But it also starts a team up with police cadet Tony Campbell. The two work together to find the murderer. But the closer Mary gets to solving the murder, the more danger she puts herself in of being the next victim.
