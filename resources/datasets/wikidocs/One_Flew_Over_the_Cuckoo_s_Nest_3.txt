The movie titled 'One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest' is directed by Miloš Forman and was released in 1975. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Jack Nicholson,Brad Dourif,Louise Fletcher,Danny DeVito,William Redfield. It has a runtime of 133 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: A petty criminal fakes insanity to serve his sentence in a mental ward rather than prison. He soon finds himself as a leader to the other patients—and an enemy to the cruel, domineering nurse who runs the ward.
