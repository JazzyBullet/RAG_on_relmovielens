The movie titled 'Jack Frost' is directed by Troy Miller and was released in 1998. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Drama, with main cast including Michael Keaton,Kelly Preston,Mark Addy,Joseph Cross,Henry Rollins. It has a runtime of 101 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: A father, who can't keep his promises, dies in a car accident. One year later, he returns as a snowman, who has the final chance to put things right with his son before he is gone forever.
