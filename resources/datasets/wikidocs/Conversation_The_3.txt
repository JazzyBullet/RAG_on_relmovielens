The movie titled 'Conversation, The' is directed by Francis Ford Coppola and was released in 1974. The genre of this movie is Drama|Mystery, with main cast including Gene Hackman,John Cazale,Allen Garfield,Frederic Forrest,Cindy Williams. It has a runtime of 113 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: Surveillance expert Harry Caul is hired by a mysterious client's brusque aide to tail a young couple. Tracking the pair through San Francisco's Union Square, Caul and his associate Stan manage to record a cryptic conversation between them. Tormented by memories of a previous case that ended badly, Caul becomes obsessed with the resulting tape, trying to determine if the couple is in danger.
