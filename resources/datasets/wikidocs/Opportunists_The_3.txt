The movie titled 'Opportunists, The' is directed by Myles Connell and was released in 2000. The genre of this movie is Crime, with main cast including Christopher Walken,Peter McDonald,Anne Pitoniak,Kate Burton,Jim Mayzik. It has a runtime of 90 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Vic is a struggling auto mechanic with a safe-cracking past and a lot of debt. His girlfriend runs a bar and offers to loan him the money she's saved for remodeling, but Vic is reluctant to take it. When a long-lost cousin from Ireland shows up on his doorstep, the two team up for one last heist.
