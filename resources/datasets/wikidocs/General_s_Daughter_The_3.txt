The movie titled 'General's Daughter, The' is directed by Simon West and was released in 1999. The genre of this movie is Drama|Thriller, with main cast including John Travolta,Madeleine Stowe,James Cromwell,Timothy Hutton,Leslie Stefanson. It has a runtime of 116 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: When the body of Army Capt. Elisabeth Campbell is found on a Georgia military base, two investigators, Warrant Officers Paul Brenner and Sara Sunhill, are ordered to solve her murder. What they uncover is anything but clear-cut. Unseemly details emerge about Campbell's life, leading to allegations of a possible military coverup of her death and the involvement of her father, Lt. Gen. Joseph Campbell.
