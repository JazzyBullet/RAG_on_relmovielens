The movie titled 'It Came from Beneath the Sea' is directed by Robert Gordon and was released in 1955. The genre of this movie is Sci-Fi, with main cast including Kenneth Tobey,Faith Domergue,Donald Curtis,Chuck Griffiths,Ian Keith. It has a runtime of 79 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: A giant octopus, whose feeding habits have been affected by radiation from H-Bomb tests, rises from the Mindanao Deep to terrorize the California Coast.
