The movie titled 'Hard Target' is directed by John Woo and was released in 1993. The genre of this movie is Action|Adventure|Crime|Thriller, with main cast including Jean-Claude Van Damme,Arnold Vosloo,Lance Henriksen,Yancy Butler,Sven-Ole Thorsen. It has a runtime of 97 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: When a woman's father goes missing, she enlists a local to aid in her search.  The pair soon discover that her father has died at the hands of a wealthy sportsman who hunts homeless men as a form of recreation.
