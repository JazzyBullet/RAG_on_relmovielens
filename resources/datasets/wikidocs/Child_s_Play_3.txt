The movie titled 'Child's Play' is directed by Tom Holland and was released in 1988. The genre of this movie is Horror, with main cast including Catherine Hicks,Chris Sarandon,Alex Vincent,Brad Dourif,Dinah Manoff. It has a runtime of 87 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: A single mother gives her son a beloved doll for his birthday, only to discover that it is possessed by the soul of a serial killer.
