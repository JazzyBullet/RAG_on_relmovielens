The movie titled 'Road Trip' is directed by Todd Phillips and was released in 2000. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Breckin Meyer,Seann William Scott,Amy Smart,Tom Green,Paulo Costanzo. It has a runtime of 93 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: After an Ithaca college student films his one-night stand with a beautiful sorority girl, he discovers one of his friends has accidentally mailed the homemade sex tape to his girlfriend. In a frenzy, he must borrow a car and hit the road in a desperate bid to intercept the tape.
