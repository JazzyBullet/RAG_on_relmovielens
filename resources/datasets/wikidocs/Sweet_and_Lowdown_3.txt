The movie titled 'Sweet and Lowdown' is directed by Woody Allen and was released in 1999. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Drama, with main cast including Sean Penn,Samantha Morton,Anthony LaPaglia,Uma Thurman,James Urbaniak. It has a runtime of 95 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: In the 1930s, jazz guitarist Emmet Ray idolizes Django Reinhardt, faces gangsters and falls in love with a mute woman.
