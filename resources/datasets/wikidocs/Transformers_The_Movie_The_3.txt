The movie titled 'Transformers: The Movie, The' is directed by Nelson Shin and was released in 1986. The genre of this movie is Action|Animation|Children's|Sci-Fi|Thriller|War, with main cast including Judd Nelson,Peter Cullen,Frank Welker,Leonard Nimoy,Orson Welles. It has a runtime of 84 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: The Autobots must stop a colossal planet-consuming robot who goes after the Autobot Matrix of Leadership.
