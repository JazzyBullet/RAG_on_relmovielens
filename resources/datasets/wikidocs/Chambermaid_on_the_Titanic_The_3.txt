The movie titled 'Chambermaid on the Titanic, The' is directed by Bigas Luna and was released in 1997. The genre of this movie is Romance, with main cast including Olivier Martinez,Aitana Sánchez-Gijón,Romane Bohringer,Didier Bezace,Aldo Maccione. It has a runtime of 100 minutes and languages used including Français,English,Magyar, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: Horty, a French foundry worker, wins a contest and is sent to see the sailing of the Titanic. In England, Marie, saying she is a chambermaid on the Titanic and cannot get a room, asks to share his room. They do, chastely; when he awakens, she is gone, but he sees her at the sailing and gets a photo of her. When he returns home, he suspects that his wife Zoe has been sleeping with Simeon, the foundry owner. Horty goes to the bar, where his friends get him drunk and he starts telling an erotic fantasy of what happened with him and Marie, drawing a larger audience each night.
