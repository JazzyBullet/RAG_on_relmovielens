The movie titled 'Love's Labour's Lost' is directed by Kenneth Branagh and was released in 2000.0. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Romance, with main cast including Kenneth Branagh,Alessandro Nivola,Adrian Lester,Matthew Lillard,Alicia Silverstone. It has a runtime of 93 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: The King of Navarre and his three companions swear a very public oath to study together and to renounce women for three years. Their honour is immediately put to the test by the arrival of the Princess of France and her three lovely companions. It's love at first sight for all concerned followed by the men's hopeless efforts to disguise their feelings.
