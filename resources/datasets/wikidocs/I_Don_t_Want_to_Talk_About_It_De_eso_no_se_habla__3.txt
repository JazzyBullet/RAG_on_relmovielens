The movie titled 'I Don't Want to Talk About It (De eso no se habla)' is directed by María Luisa Bemberg and was released in 1993. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Marcello Mastroianni,Luisina Brando,Alejandra Podestá,Betiana Blum,Roberto Carnaghi. It has a runtime of 106 minutes and languages used including Español, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: In an Argentine village in the 1930s, the willful Leonor discovers that her beloved daughter, Charlotte, has dwarfism. Attempting to let her have as normal a childhood as possible, Leonor never discusses Charlotte's lack of stature, nor lets anyone else in the village ever mention it. As Charlotte grows up into a smart and self-possessed young woman, local eccentric Ludovico, who collects miniatures, becomes smitten with her.
