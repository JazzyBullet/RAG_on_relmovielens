The movie titled 'Sonic Outlaws' is directed by Craig Baldwin and was released in 1995. The genre of this movie is Documentary, with main cast including Lloyd Dunn,Chris Grigg,Mark Hosler,Don Joyce,Doug Kahn. It has a runtime of 87 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: Within days of the release of Negativland's clever parody of U2 and Casey Kasem, recording industry giant Island Records descended upon the band with a battery of lawyers intent on erasing the piece from the history of rock music.  Craig "Tribulation 99" Baldwin follows this and other intellectual property controversies across the contemporary arts scene. Playful and ironic, his cut-and-paste collage-essay surveys the prospects for an "electronic folk culture" in the midst of an increasingly commodified corporate media landscape.
