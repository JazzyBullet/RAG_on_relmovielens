The movie titled 'Spy Hard' is directed by Rick Friedberg and was released in 1996. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Leslie Nielsen,Nicollette Sheridan,Charles Durning,Marcia Gay Harden,Barry Bostwick. It has a runtime of 81 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: The evil Gen. Rancor has his sights set on world domination, and only one man can stop him: Dick Steele, also known as Agent WD-40. Rancor needs to obtain a computer circuit for the missile that he is planning to fire, so Steele teams up with Veronique Ukrinsky, a KGB agent whose father designed the chip. Together they try to locate the evil mastermind's headquarters, where Veronique's father and several other hostages are being held.
