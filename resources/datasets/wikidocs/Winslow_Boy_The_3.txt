The movie titled 'Winslow Boy, The' is directed by David Mamet and was released in 1999. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Rebecca Pidgeon,Gemma Jones,Nigel Hawthorne,Sarah Flind,Colin Stinton. It has a runtime of 104 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of G. The plot summary is as follows: Early 20th century England: while toasting his daughter Catherine's engagement, Arthur Winslow learns the royal naval academy expelled his 14-year-old son, Ronnie, for stealing five shillings. Father asks son if it is true; when the lad denies it, Arthur risks fortune, health, domestic peace, and Catherine's prospects to pursue justice.
