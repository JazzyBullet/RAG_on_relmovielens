The movie titled 'Misery' is directed by Rob Reiner and was released in 1990. The genre of this movie is Horror, with main cast including James Caan,Kathy Bates,Richard Farnsworth,Frances Sternhagen,Lauren Bacall. It has a runtime of 107 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: After stalking and saving the life of her favorite fiction author in a car accident, his  manic obsessor holds him captive in her remote Colorado home then forces him to write back to life the popular literary character he killed off.
