The movie titled 'Raging Bull' is directed by Martin Scorsese and was released in 1980. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Robert De Niro,Cathy Moriarty,Joe Pesci,Frank Vincent,Nicholas Colasanto. It has a runtime of 129 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: The life of boxer Jake LaMotta, whose violence and temper that led him to the top in the ring destroyed his life outside of it.
