The movie titled 'Murder!' is directed by Alfred Hitchcock and was released in 1930. The genre of this movie is Mystery|Thriller, with main cast including Herbert Marshall,Edward Chapman,Esme Percy,Norah Baring,Phyllis Konstam. It has a runtime of 102 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: When a woman is convicted of murder, one of the jurors selected to serve on the murder-trial jury believes the accused, an aspiring actress, is innocent of the crime and takes it upon himself to apprehend the real killer.
