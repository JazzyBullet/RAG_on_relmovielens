The movie titled 'Warriors of Virtue' is directed by Ronny Yu and was released in 1997. The genre of this movie is Action|Adventure|Children's|Fantasy, with main cast including Angus Macfadyen,Mario Yedidia,Marley Shelton,Chao Li Chi,Jack Tate. It has a runtime of 103 minutes and languages used including 普通话,English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: A young man, Ryan, suffering from a disability, wishes to join the other kids from his schools football team. During an initiation rite, Ryan is swept away through a whirlpool to the land of Tao. There he is hunted by the evil Lord Komodo, who desires the boy as a key to enter the real world. Ryan is rescued by the protectors of Tao, five humanoid kangaroos, each embued with the five elements and virtues. Ryan learns his valuable lesson while saving the land of Tao.
