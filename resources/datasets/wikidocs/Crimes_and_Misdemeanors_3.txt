The movie titled 'Crimes and Misdemeanors' is directed by Woody Allen and was released in 1989. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Woody Allen,Martin Landau,Mia Farrow,Alan Alda,Anjelica Huston. It has a runtime of 104 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13.
