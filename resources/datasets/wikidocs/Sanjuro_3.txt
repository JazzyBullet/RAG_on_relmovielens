The movie titled 'Sanjuro' is directed by Akira Kurosawa and was released in 1962. The genre of this movie is Action|Adventure, with main cast including Toshirō Mifune,Tatsuya Nakadai,Keiju Kobayashi,Yūzō Kayama,Reiko Dan. It has a runtime of 96 minutes and languages used including 日本語, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: Toshiro Mifune swaggers and snarls to brilliant comic effect in Kurosawa's tightly paced, beautifully composed "Sanjuro." In this companion piece and sequel to "Yojimbo," jaded samurai Sanjuro helps an idealistic group of young warriors weed out their clan's evil influences, and in the process turns their image of a proper samurai on its ear.
