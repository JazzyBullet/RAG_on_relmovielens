The movie titled 'Raise the Red Lantern' is directed by Zhang Yimou and was released in 1991. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Gong Li,Ma Jingwu,He Saifei,Cao Cuifen,Kong Lin. It has a runtime of 125 minutes and languages used including 普通话, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: China in the 1920s. After her father's death, 19 year old Songlian is forced to marry the much older lord of a powerful family. With three wives already, each living in a separate house within the great castle, there is fierce competition for his attention and the privileges that are gained. This competition gets out of hand.
