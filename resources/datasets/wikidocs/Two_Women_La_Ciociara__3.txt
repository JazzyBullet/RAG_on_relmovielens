The movie titled 'Two Women (La Ciociara)' is directed by Vittorio De Sica and was released in 1960.0. The genre of this movie is Drama|War, with main cast including Sophia Loren,Jean-Paul Belmondo,Raf Vallone,Eleonora Brown,Carlo Ninchi. It has a runtime of 100 minutes and languages used including Deutsch,Italiano, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: Widowed shopkeeper Cesira and her 13-year-old daughter Rosetta flee from the allied bombs in Rome during the second World War; they travel to the remote village where Cesira was born. During their journey and in the village and onward, the mother does everything she can to protect Rosetta. Meanwhile, a sensitive young intellectual, Michele, falls in love with Cesira.
