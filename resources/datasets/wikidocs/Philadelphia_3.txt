The movie titled 'Philadelphia' is directed by Jonathan Demme and was released in 1993. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Tom Hanks,Denzel Washington,Jason Robards,Mary Steenburgen,Antonio Banderas. It has a runtime of 126 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: Two competing lawyers join forces to sue a prestigious law firm for AIDS discrimination. As their unlikely friendship develops their courage overcomes the prejudice and corruption of their powerful adversaries.
