The movie titled 'Back to School' is directed by Alan Metter and was released in 1986. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Rodney Dangerfield,Sally Kellerman,Burt Young,Keith Gordon,Robert Downey Jr.. It has a runtime of 96 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: Self-made millionaire Thornton Melon decides to get a better education and enrolls at his son Jason's college. While Jason tries to fit in with his fellow students, Thornton struggles to gain his son's respect, giving way to hilarious antics.
