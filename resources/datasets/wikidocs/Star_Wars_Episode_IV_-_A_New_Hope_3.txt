The movie titled 'Star Wars: Episode IV - A New Hope' is directed by George Lucas and was released in 1977. The genre of this movie is Action|Adventure|Fantasy|Sci-Fi, with main cast including Mark Hamill,Harrison Ford,Carrie Fisher,Peter Cushing,Alec Guinness. It has a runtime of 121 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: Princess Leia is captured and held hostage by the evil Imperial forces in their effort to take over the galactic Empire. Venturesome Luke Skywalker and dashing captain Han Solo team together with the loveable robot duo R2-D2 and C-3PO to rescue the beautiful princess and restore peace and justice in the Empire.
