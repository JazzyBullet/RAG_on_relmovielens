The movie titled 'Cruel Intentions' is directed by Roger Kumble and was released in 1999. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Ryan Phillippe,Sarah Michelle Gellar,Reese Witherspoon,Selma Blair,Louise Fletcher. It has a runtime of 98 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Slaking a thirst for dangerous games, Kathryn challenges her stepbrother, Sebastian, to deflower their headmaster's daughter before the summer ends. If he succeeds, the prize is the chance to bed Kathryn. But if he loses, Kathryn will claim his most prized possession.
