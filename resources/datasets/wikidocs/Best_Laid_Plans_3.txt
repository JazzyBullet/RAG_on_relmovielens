The movie titled 'Best Laid Plans' is directed by Mike Barker and was released in 1999. The genre of this movie is Crime|Drama, with main cast including Alessandro Nivola,Reese Witherspoon,Josh Brolin,Gene Wolande,Jonathan McMurtry. It has a runtime of 92 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Rich, successful Bryce meets beautiful Lissa at a bar one night and invites her back to his house, not suspecting for a moment that Lissa isn't really who she seems. What unfolds next is a dangerous, tangled web of double-crosses and seduction.
