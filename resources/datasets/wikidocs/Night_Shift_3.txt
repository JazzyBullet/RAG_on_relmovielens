The movie titled 'Night Shift' is directed by Ron Howard and was released in 1982. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Henry Winkler,Michael Keaton,Shelley Long,Gina Hecht,Pat Corley. It has a runtime of 106 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: A nebbish of a morgue attendant gets shunted back to the night shift where he is shackled with an obnoxious neophyte partner who dreams of the "one great idea" for success. His life takes a bizarre turn when a prostitute neighbor complains about the loss of her pimp. His partner, upon hearing the situation, suggests that they fill that opening themselves using the morgue at night.
