The movie titled 'Much Ado About Nothing' is directed by Kenneth Branagh and was released in 1993. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Romance, with main cast including Emma Thompson,Kenneth Branagh,Robert Sean Leonard,Kate Beckinsale,Denzel Washington. It has a runtime of 111 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: In this Shakespearean farce, Hero and her groom-to-be, Claudio, team up with Claudio's commanding officer, Don Pedro, the week before their wedding to hatch a matchmaking scheme. Their targets are sharp-witted duo Benedick and Beatrice -- a tough task indeed, considering their corresponding distaste for love and each other. Meanwhile, meddling Don John plots to ruin the wedding.
