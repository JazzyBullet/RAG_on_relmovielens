The movie titled 'Dreamlife of Angels, The (La Vie rêvée des anges)' is directed by Erick Zonca and was released in 1998.0. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Élodie Bouchez,Natacha Régnier,Grégoire Colin,Patrick Mercado,Jo Prestia. It has a runtime of 113 minutes and languages used including Français, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Isa and Marie bond while working in a French sweatshop and soon begin sharing an apartment that Marie is watching for a hospitalized mother and daughter. Marie, hoping to avoid a life of struggle and poverty, takes up with Chriss, a nightclub owner whose most attractive asset is his money. Isa recognizes the ultimate futility of the relationship and tries to keep Marie away from him, but her interference puts their friendship at risk.
