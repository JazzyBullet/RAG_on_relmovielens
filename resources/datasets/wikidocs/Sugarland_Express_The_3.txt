The movie titled 'Sugarland Express, The' is directed by Steven Spielberg and was released in 1974.0. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Goldie Hawn,Ben Johnson,Michael Sacks,William Atherton,Gregory Walcott. It has a runtime of 110 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: Married small-time crooks Lou-Jean and Clovis Poplin lose their baby to the state of Texas and resolve to do whatever it takes to get him back. Lou-Jean gets Clovis out of jail, and the two steal their son from his foster home, in addition to taking a highway patrolman hostage. As a massive dragnet starts to pursue them across Texas, the couple become unlikely folk heroes and even start to bond with the captive policeman.
