The movie titled 'Abyss, The' is directed by James Cameron and was released in 1989. The genre of this movie is Action|Adventure|Sci-Fi|Thriller, with main cast including Ed Harris,Mary Elizabeth Mastrantonio,Michael Biehn,Leo Burmester,Todd Graff. It has a runtime of 140 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: A civilian oil rig crew is recruited to conduct a search and rescue effort when a nuclear submarine mysteriously sinks. One diver soon finds himself on a spectacular odyssey 25,000 feet below the ocean's surface where he confronts a mysterious force that has the power to change the world or destroy it.
