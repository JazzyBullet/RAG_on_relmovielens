The movie titled 'Little Buddha' is directed by Bernardo Bertolucci and was released in 1993. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Keanu Reeves,Bridget Fonda,Chris Isaak,Ruocheng Ying,Alex Wiesendanger. It has a runtime of 140 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: After the death of Lama Dorje, Tibetan Buddhist monks find three children — one American and two Nepalese — who may be the rebirth of their great teacher.
