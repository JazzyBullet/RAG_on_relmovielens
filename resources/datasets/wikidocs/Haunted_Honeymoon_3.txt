The movie titled 'Haunted Honeymoon' is directed by Gene Wilder and was released in 1986. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Gene Wilder,Gilda Radner,Dom DeLuise,Jonathan Pryce,Eve Ferret. It has a runtime of 82 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: Radio personalities Larry Abbot and Vickie Pearle are stars of a mystery show. Since they announced their engagement, Larry has been plagued by speech problems and, seeking out an unconventional cure, he returns to his boyhood home, a mansion in the countryside, bringing Vickie along. Larry reunites with numerous family members, but discovers that there are sinister things afoot within the walls of the creepy estate.
