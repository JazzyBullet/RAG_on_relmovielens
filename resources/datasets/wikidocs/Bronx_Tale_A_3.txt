The movie titled 'Bronx Tale, A' is directed by Robert De Niro and was released in 1993. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Robert De Niro,Chazz Palminteri,Lillo Brancato,Francis Capra,Taral Hicks. It has a runtime of 121 minutes and languages used including English,Italiano, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Set in the Bronx during the tumultuous 1960s, an adolescent boy is torn between his honest, working-class father and a violent yet charismatic crime boss. Complicating matters is the youngster's growing attraction - forbidden in his neighborhood - for a beautiful black girl.
