The movie titled 'Night of the Living Dead' is directed by George A. Romero and was released in 1968. The genre of this movie is Horror|Sci-Fi, with main cast including Judith O'Dea,Duane Jones,Marilyn Eastman,Karl Hardman,Judith Ridley. It has a runtime of 96 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: A disparate group of individuals takes refuge in an abandoned house when corpses begin to leave the graveyard in search of fresh human bodies to devour. The pragmatic Ben does his best to control the situation, but when the murderous zombies surround the house, the other survivors begin to panic.
