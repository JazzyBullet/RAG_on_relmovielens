The movie titled 'Bull Durham' is directed by Ron Shelton and was released in 1988. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Kevin Costner,Susan Sarandon,Tim Robbins,Trey Wilson,Robert Wuhl. It has a runtime of 108 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Veteran catcher Crash Davis is brought to the minor league Durham Bulls to help their up and coming pitching prospect, "Nuke" Laloosh. Their relationship gets off to a rocky start and is further complicated when baseball groupie Annie Savoy sets her sights on the two men.
