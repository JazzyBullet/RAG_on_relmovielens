It has a runtime of 119 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: This movie chronicles the life and times of R. Crumb. Through interviews with his mother, two brothers, wife, and ex-girlfriends, as well as selections from his vast quantity of graphic art, we are treated to a darkly comic ride through one man's subconscious mind.
