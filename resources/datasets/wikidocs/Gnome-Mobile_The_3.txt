The movie titled 'Gnome-Mobile, The' is directed by Robert Stevenson and was released in 1967. The genre of this movie is Children's, with main cast including Walter Brennan,Matthew Garber,Karen Dotrice,Richard Deacon,Sean McClory. It has a runtime of 85 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of G. The plot summary is as follows: An eccentric millionaire and his grandchildren are embroiled in the plights of some forest gnomes who are searching for the rest of their tribe. While helping them, the millionaire is suspected of being crazy because he's seeing gnomes! He's committed, and the niece and nephew and the gnomes have to find him and free him.
