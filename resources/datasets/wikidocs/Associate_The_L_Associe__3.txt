The movie titled 'Associate, The (L'Associe)' is directed by René Gainville and was released in 1979. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Michel Serrault,Claudine Auger,Catherine Alric,Judith Magre,Bernard Haller. It has a runtime of 94 minutes and languages used including Français, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: An unemployed investor creates a fictious business partner to attempt to improve business. Eventually, his creation gets out of control as his business becomes successful and his wife announces that she is in love with the partner and his son wishes the partner was his father -- although no one has ever seen him. To regain control, the man decides to "kill" his imaginary partner and is arrested for the murder.
