The movie titled 'Doctor Zhivago' is directed by David Lean and was released in 1965. The genre of this movie is Drama|Romance|War, with main cast including Omar Sharif,Julie Christie,Geraldine Chaplin,Rod Steiger,Alec Guinness. It has a runtime of 200 minutes and languages used including English,Pусский, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: The life of a Russian physician and poet who, although married to another, falls in love with a political activist's wife and experiences hardship during World War I and then the October Revolution.
