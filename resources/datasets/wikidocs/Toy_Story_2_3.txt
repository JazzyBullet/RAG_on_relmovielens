The movie titled 'Toy Story 2' is directed by John Lasseter and was released in 1999. The genre of this movie is Animation|Children's|Comedy, with main cast including Tom Hanks,Tim Allen,Joan Cusack,Kelsey Grammer,Don Rickles. It has a runtime of 92 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of G. The plot summary is as follows: Andy heads off to Cowboy Camp, leaving his toys to their own devices. Things shift into high gear when an obsessive toy collector named Al McWhiggen, owner of Al's Toy Barn kidnaps Woody. Andy's toys mount a daring rescue mission, Buzz Lightyear meets his match and Woody has to decide where he and his heart truly belong.
