The movie titled 'Losing Isaiah' is directed by Stephen Gyllenhaal and was released in 1995. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Jessica Lange,Halle Berry,David Strathairn,Cuba Gooding Jr.,Daisy Eagan. It has a runtime of 106 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Khaila Richards, a crack-addicted single mother, accidentally leaves her baby in a dumpster while high and returns the next day in a panic to find he is missing. In reality, the baby has been adopted by a warm-hearted social worker, Margaret Lewin, and her husband, Charles. Years later, Khaila has gone through rehab and holds a steady job. After learning that her child is still alive, she challenges Margaret for the custody.
