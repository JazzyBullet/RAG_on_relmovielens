The movie titled 'Yankee Zulu' is directed by Gray Hofmeyr and was released in 1993. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Drama, with main cast including Leon Schuster,John Matshikiza,Wilson Dunster,Terri Treas,Michelle Bowes. It has a runtime of 100 minutes and languages used including Afrikaans,English, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: Two South African boys, one white, Rhino, and one black, Zulu, go their separate ways after an incident. Many years later, they meet up again as adults, when one, after living for years in the United States, is now a wanted criminal. The two end up being a part of a madcap chase involving a check for a large amount of lottery money, pursued by Gen. Diehard and Rhino's ex-wife Rowena, who was the cause of the rift between the two protagonists.
