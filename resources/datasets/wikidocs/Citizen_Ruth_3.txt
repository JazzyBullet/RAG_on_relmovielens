The movie titled 'Citizen Ruth' is directed by Alexander Payne and was released in 1996. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Drama, with main cast including Laura Dern,Swoosie Kurtz,Kurtwood Smith,Mary Kay Place,Kelly Preston. It has a runtime of 104 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: "Citizen Ruth" is the story of Ruth Stoops, a woman who nobody even noticed -- until she got pregnant. Now, everyone wants a piece of her. The film is a comedy about one woman caught in the ultimate tug-of-war: a clash of wild, noisy, ridiculous people that rapidly dissolves into a media circus.
