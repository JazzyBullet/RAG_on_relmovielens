The movie titled 'When a Man Loves a Woman' is directed by Luis Mandoki and was released in 1994. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Andy García,Meg Ryan,Tina Majorino,Philip Seymour Hoffman,Lauren Tom. It has a runtime of 126 minutes and languages used including English,Tiếng Việt, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: An airline pilot and his wife are forced to face the consequences of her alcoholism when her addictions threaten her life and their daughter's safety. While the woman enters detox, her husband must face the truth of his enabling behavior.
