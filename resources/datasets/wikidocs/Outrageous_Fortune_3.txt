The genre of this movie is Comedy|Mystery, with main cast including Shelley Long,Bette Midler,Peter Coyote,Robert Prosky,John Schuck. The plot summary is as follows: Refined actress Lauren Ames finally has a chance to study with the great theatre professor Stanislav Korzenowski. Sandy Brozinsky, a brash, loud actress, decides through happenstance to also study with Korzenowski. The two women end up dating the same man (who turns out to be a double agent) and follow him across the country to force him to choose between them.
