The movie titled 'Son of Flubber' is directed by Robert Stevenson and was released in 1963. The genre of this movie is Children's|Comedy, with main cast including Fred MacMurray,Nancy Olson,Keenan Wynn,Tommy Kirk,Ed Wynn. It has a runtime of 100 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of G. The plot summary is as follows: Beleaguered professor Ned Brainard has already run into a pile of misfortunes with his discovery of the super-elastic substance "Flubber." Now he hopes to have better luck with a gravity-busting derivative he's dubbed "Flubbergas." Ned's experiments, constantly hampered by government obstruction, earn the consternation of his wife, Betsy. But a game-winning modification to a football uniform may help Ned make the case for his fantastic new invention.
