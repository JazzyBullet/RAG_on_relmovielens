The movie titled 'Instinct' is directed by Jon Turteltaub and was released in 1999. The genre of this movie is Drama|Thriller, with main cast including Anthony Hopkins,Cuba Gooding Jr.,Donald Sutherland,Maura Tierney,George Dzundza. It has a runtime of 124 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: In a prison for the criminally insane, deranged anthropologist Ethan Powell is set to be examined by a bright young psychiatrist, Theo Caulder. Driven by ambition and a hunger for the truth, Caulder will eventually risk everything—even put his very life on the line—in a harrowing attempt to understand the bizarre actions of this madman.
