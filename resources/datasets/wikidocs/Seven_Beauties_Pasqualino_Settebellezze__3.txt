The movie titled 'Seven Beauties (Pasqualino Settebellezze)' is directed by Lina Wertmüller and was released in 1975. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Drama, with main cast including Giancarlo Giannini,Fernando Rey,Shirley Stoler,Elena Fiore,Roberto Herlitzka. It has a runtime of 116 minutes and languages used including Deutsch,Español,English,Italiano, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Pasqualino Frafuso, known in Naples as "Pasqualino Seven Beauties" is a petty thief who lives off of the profits of his seven sisters while claiming to protect their honor at any cost, Pasqualino is arrested for murder and later sent to fight in the army after committing sexual assault. The Germans capture him and he gets sent to a concentration camp where he plots to make his escape by seducing a German officer.
