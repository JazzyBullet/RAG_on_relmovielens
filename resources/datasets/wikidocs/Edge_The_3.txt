The plot summary is as follows: The plane carrying wealthy Charles Morse crashes down in the Alaskan wilderness. However, his biggest obstacle might not be the elements, or even the Kodiak bear stalking them -- it could be Robert, whom Charles suspects is having an affair with his wife and would not mind seeing him dead.
