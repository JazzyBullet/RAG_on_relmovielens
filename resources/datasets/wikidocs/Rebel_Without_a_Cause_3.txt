The movie titled 'Rebel Without a Cause' is directed by Nicholas Ray and was released in 1955.0. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including James Dean,Natalie Wood,Sal Mineo,Jim Backus,Ann Doran. It has a runtime of 111 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: After moving to a new town, troublemaking teen Jim Stark is supposed to have a clean slate, although being the new kid in town brings its own problems. While searching for some stability, Stark forms a bond with a disturbed classmate, Plato, and falls for local girl Judy. However, Judy is the girlfriend of neighborhood tough, Buzz. When Buzz violently confronts Jim and challenges him to a drag race, the new kid's real troubles begin.
