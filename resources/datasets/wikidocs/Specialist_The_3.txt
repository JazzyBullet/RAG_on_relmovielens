The movie titled 'Specialist, The' is directed by Luis Llosa and was released in 1994. The genre of this movie is Action, with main cast including Sylvester Stallone,Sharon Stone,James Woods,Rod Steiger,Eric Roberts. It has a runtime of 110 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: May Munro is a woman obsessed with getting revenge on the people who murdered her parents when she was still a girl. She hires Ray Quick, a retired explosives expert to kill her parent's killers. When Ned Trent, embittered ex-partner of Quick's is assigned to protect one of Quick's potential victims, a deadly game of cat and mouse ensues.
