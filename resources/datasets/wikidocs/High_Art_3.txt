The movie titled 'High Art' is directed by Lisa Cholodenko and was released in 1998. The genre of this movie is Drama|Romance, with main cast including Radha Mitchell,Gabriel Mann,Ally Sheedy,Patricia Clarkson,Charis Michelsen.
