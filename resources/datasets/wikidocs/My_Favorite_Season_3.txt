The movie titled 'My Favorite Season' is directed by André Téchiné and was released in 1993. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Daniel Auteuil,Marthe Villalonga,Catherine Deneuve,Jean-Pierre Bouvier,Chiara Mastroianni. The plot summary is as follows: Elderly woman Berthe leaves her house to live with her daughter Emilie. Memories will resurface and impact both Berthe's destiny and the strange relationship between Emilie and Antoine.
