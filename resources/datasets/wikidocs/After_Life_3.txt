The movie titled 'After Life' is directed by Hirokazu Kore-eda and was released in 1998.0. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Arata Iura,Erika Oda,Susumu Terajima,Takashi Naito,Kei Tani. The plot summary is as follows: On a cold Monday morning, a group of counselors clock in at an old-fashioned social services office. Their task is to interview the recently deceased, record their personal details, then, over the course of the week, assist them in choosing a single memory to keep for eternity.
