The movie titled 'Cutter's Way' is directed by Ivan Passer and was released in 1981. The genre of this movie is Drama|Thriller, with main cast including Jeff Bridges,John Heard,Lisa Eichhorn,Ann Dusenberry,Stephen Elliott. It has a runtime of 105 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Richard spots a man dumping a body, and decides to expose the man he thinks is the culprit with his friend Alex Cutter.
