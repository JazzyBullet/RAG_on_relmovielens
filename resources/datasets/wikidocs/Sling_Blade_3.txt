The movie titled 'Sling Blade' is directed by Billy Bob Thornton and was released in 1996. The genre of this movie is Drama|Thriller, with main cast including Billy Bob Thornton,Dwight Yoakam,J.T. Walsh,John Ritter,Lucas Black. It has a runtime of 135 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Karl Childers, a mentally disabled man, has been in the custody of the state mental hospital since the age of 12 for killing his mother and her lover. Although thoroughly institutionalized, he is deemed fit to be released into the outside world.
