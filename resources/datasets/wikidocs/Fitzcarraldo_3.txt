The movie titled 'Fitzcarraldo' is directed by Werner Herzog and was released in 1982. The genre of this movie is Adventure|Drama, with main cast including Klaus Kinski,Claudia Cardinale,José Lewgoy,Miguel Ángel Fuentes,Paul Hittscher. It has a runtime of 157 minutes and languages used including English,Deutsch,Italiano,Español, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: Fitzcarraldo is a dreamer who plans to build an opera house in Iquitos, in the Peruvian Amazon, so, in order to finance his project, he embarks on an epic adventure to collect rubber, a very profitable product, in a remote and unexplored region of the rainforest.
