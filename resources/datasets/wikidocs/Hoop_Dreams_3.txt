The movie titled 'Hoop Dreams' is directed by Steve James and was released in 1994.0. The genre of this movie is Documentary, with main cast including William Gates,Arthur Agee,Gene Pingatore,Steve James,Dick Vitale. It has a runtime of 174 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: Every school day, African-American teenagers William Gates and Arthur Agee travel 90 minutes each way from inner-city Chicago to St. Joseph High School in Westchester, Illinois, a predominately white suburban school well-known for the excellence of its basketball program. Gates and Agee dream of NBA stardom, and with the support of their close-knit families, they battle the social and physical obstacles that stand in their way. This acclaimed documentary was shot over the course of five years.
