The movie titled 'Seven (Se7en)' is directed by David Fincher and was released in 1995. The genre of this movie is Crime|Thriller, with main cast including Morgan Freeman,Brad Pitt,Gwyneth Paltrow,John Cassini,Peter Crombie. It has a runtime of 127 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Two homicide detectives are on a desperate hunt for a serial killer whose crimes are based on the "seven deadly sins" in this dark and haunting film that takes viewers from the tortured remains of one victim to the next. The seasoned Det. Sommerset researches each sin in an effort to get inside the killer's mind, while his novice partner, Mills, scoffs at his efforts to unravel the case.
