The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Amy Irving,Antônio Fagundes,Alexandre Borges,Débora Bloch,Drica Moraes. The plot summary is as follows: Bossa Nova is a romantic comedy that enfolds many love convergences and divergences, in today's Rio de Janeiro. It's in the city's “useless landscape” that the lonely hearts of nine characters will cross destinies and they'll discover new passions. The meeting place for these journeys is a charming English teacher, Mary Ann Simpson, former airline stewardess, who's been living in Rio for two years, since she became a widow, and the newly separated Pedro Paulo. They approach each other without a hint that their lives will be linked by many characters and incidents.
