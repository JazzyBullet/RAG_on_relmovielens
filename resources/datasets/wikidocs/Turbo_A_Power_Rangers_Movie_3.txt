The movie titled 'Turbo: A Power Rangers Movie' is directed by Shuki Levy,David Winning and was released in 1997. The genre of this movie is Action|Adventure|Children's, with main cast including Blake Foster,Steve Cardenas,Austin St. John,Amy Jo Johnson,Catherine Sutherland. It has a runtime of 99 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: The legendary Power Rangers must stop the evil space pirate Divatox from releasing the powerful Maligore from his volcanic imprisonment on the island of Muranthias, where only the kindly wizard Lerigot has the key to release him. The hope of victory lies in the Ranger's incredible new Turbo powers and powerful Turbo Zords.
