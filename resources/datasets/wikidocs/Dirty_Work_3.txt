The movie titled 'Dirty Work' is directed by Bob Saget and was released in 1998. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Norm Macdonald,Artie Lange,Jack Warden,Traylor Howard,Christopher McDonald. It has a runtime of 82 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: Unemployed and recently dumped, Mitch and his buddy Sam start a revenge-for-hire business to raise the $50,000 that Sam's father needs to get a heart transplant.
