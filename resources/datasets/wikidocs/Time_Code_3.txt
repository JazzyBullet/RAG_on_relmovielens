The movie titled 'Time Code' is directed by Mike Figgis and was released in 2000.0. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Xander Berkeley,Golden Brooks,Saffron Burrows,Viveka Davis,Richard Edson. It has a runtime of 97 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: A production company begins casting for its next feature, and an up-and-coming actress named Rose tries to manipulate her filmmaker boyfriend, Alex, into giving her a screen test. Alex's wife, Emma, knows about the affair and is considering divorce, while Rose's girlfriend secretly spies on her and attempts to sabotage the relationship. The four storylines in the film were each shot in one take and are shown simultaneously, each taking up a quarter of the screen.
