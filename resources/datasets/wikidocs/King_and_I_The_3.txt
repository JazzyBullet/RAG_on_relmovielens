The movie titled 'King and I, The' is directed by Walter Lang and was released in 1956. The genre of this movie is Musical, with main cast including Deborah Kerr,Yul Brynner,Rita Moreno,Martin Benson,Terry Saunders. It has a runtime of 133 minutes and languages used including English,Italiano,ภาษาไทย, with a Certificate rating of G. The plot summary is as follows: Widowed Welsh mother Anna Loenowens becomes a governess and English tutor to the wives and many children of the stubborn King Mongkut of Siam. Anna and the King have a clash of personalities as she works to teach the royal family about the English language, customs and etiquette, and rushes to prepare a party for a group of European diplomats who must change their opinions about the King.
