The movie titled 'Gattaca' is directed by Andrew Niccol and was released in 1997. The genre of this movie is Drama|Sci-Fi|Thriller, with main cast including Ethan Hawke,Uma Thurman,Jude Law,Alan Arkin,Loren Dean. It has a runtime of 106 minutes and languages used including English,Esperanto, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: In a future society in the era of indefinite eugenics, humans are set on a life course depending on their DNA. Young Vincent Freeman is born with a condition that would prevent him from space travel, yet is determined to infiltrate the GATTACA space program.
