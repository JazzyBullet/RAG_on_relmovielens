The movie titled 'Starship Troopers' is directed by Paul Verhoeven and was released in 1997. The genre of this movie is Action|Adventure|Sci-Fi|War, with main cast including Casper Van Dien,Dina Meyer,Denise Richards,Jake Busey,Neil Patrick Harris. It has a runtime of 129 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Set in the future, the story follows a young soldier named Johnny Rico and his exploits in the Mobile Infantry. Rico's military career progresses from recruit to non-commissioned officer and finally to officer against the backdrop of an interstellar war between mankind and an arachnoid species known as "the Bugs".
