The movie titled 'Freaky Friday' is directed by Gary Nelson and was released in 1976.0. The genre of this movie is Children's|Comedy, with main cast including Jodie Foster,Barbara Harris,John Astin,Patsy Kelly,Dick Van Patten. It has a runtime of 95 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of G. The plot summary is as follows: School girl Annabel is hassled by her mother, and Mrs. Andrews is annoyed with her daughter, Annabel. They both think that the other has an easy life. On a normal Friday morning, both complain about each other and wish they could have the easy life of their daughter/mother for just one day and their wishes come true as a bit of magic puts Annabel in Mrs. Andrews' body and vice versa. They both have a Freaky Friday.
