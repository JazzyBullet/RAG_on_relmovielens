The movie titled 'Klute' is directed by Alan J. Pakula and was released in 1971. The genre of this movie is Drama|Mystery, with main cast including Jane Fonda,Donald Sutherland,Charles Cioffi,Roy Scheider,Dorothy Tristan. It has a runtime of 114 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: A high-priced call girl is forced to depend on a reluctant private eye when she is stalked by a psychopath.
