The movie titled 'Strange Days' is directed by Kathryn Bigelow and was released in 1995. The genre of this movie is Action|Crime|Sci-Fi, with main cast including Ralph Fiennes,Angela Bassett,Juliette Lewis,Tom Sizemore,Michael Wincott. It has a runtime of 145 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Former policeman Lenny Nero has moved into a more lucrative trade: the illegal sale of virtual reality-like recordings that allow users to experience the emotions and past experiences of others. While they typically contain tawdry incidents, Nero is shocked when he receives one showing a murder.
