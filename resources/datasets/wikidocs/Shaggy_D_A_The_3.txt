The movie titled 'Shaggy D.A., The' is directed by Robert Stevenson and was released in 1976. The genre of this movie is Children's|Comedy, with main cast including Dean Jones,Suzanne Pleshette,Tim Conway,Keenan Wynn,Jo Anne Worley. It has a runtime of 91 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of G. The plot summary is as follows: Wilby Daniels, a successful lawyer running for District Attorney, suddenly finds himself being transformed into an English sheepdog. Somehow he has to keep his change a secret and find just what is causing it, all the while eluding the local dog catcher.
