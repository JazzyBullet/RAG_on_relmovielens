The movie titled 'Jakob the Liar' is directed by Peter Kassovitz and was released in 1999.0. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Robin Williams,Liev Schreiber,Hannah Taylor-Gordon,Alan Arkin,Michael Jeter. It has a runtime of 120 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: In 1944 Poland, a Jewish shop keeper named Jakob is summoned to ghetto headquarters after being caught out after curfew. While waiting for the German Kommondant, Jakob overhears a German radio broadcast about Russian troop movements. Returned to the ghetto, the shopkeeper shares his information with a friend and then rumors fly that there is a secret radio within the ghetto.
