The movie titled 'Blowup' is directed by Michelangelo Antonioni and was released in 1966. The genre of this movie is Drama|Mystery, with main cast including David Hemmings,Vanessa Redgrave,Sarah Miles,John Castle,Veruschka von Lehndorff. It has a runtime of 112 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: A successful mod photographer in London whose world is bounded by fashion, pop music, marijuana, and easy sex, feels his life is boring and despairing. But in the course of a single day he unknowingly captures a death on film.
