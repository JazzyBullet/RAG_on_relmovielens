The movie titled 'Surf Nazis Must Die' is directed by Peter George and was released in 1987. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Gail Neely,Robert Harden,Barry Brenner,Dawn Wildsmith,Michael Sonye. It has a runtime of 81 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R.
