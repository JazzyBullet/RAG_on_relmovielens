The movie titled 'Rough Magic' is directed by Clare Peploe and was released in 1995. The genre of this movie is Drama|Romance, with main cast including Bridget Fonda,Russell Crowe,Jim Broadbent,D.W. Moffett,Kenneth Mars. It has a runtime of 100 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13.
