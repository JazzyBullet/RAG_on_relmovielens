The movie titled 'Mighty, The' is directed by Peter Chelsom and was released in 1998. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Sharon Stone,Elden Henson,Kieran Culkin,Gena Rowlands,Harry Dean Stanton. It has a runtime of 100 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: Kevin, an intelligent guy helps out Maxwell to improve his reading skills. In return, Kevin wants Maxwell to take him out places since he is not authorized to go out. Being the social outcasts of the town, Kevin and Maxwell come to realize that they are similar to each other and accept that they are "freaks" and nothing will stop them.
