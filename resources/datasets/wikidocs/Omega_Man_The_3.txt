The movie titled 'Omega Man, The' is directed by Boris Sagal and was released in 1971. The genre of this movie is Sci-Fi, with main cast including Charlton Heston,Anthony Zerbe,Rosalind Cash,Paul Koslo,Eric Laneuville. It has a runtime of 98 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: Due to an experimental vaccine, Dr. Robert Neville is the only human survivor of an apocalyptic war waged with biological weapons. Besides him, only a few hundred deformed, nocturnal people remain - sensitive to light, and homicidally psychotic.
