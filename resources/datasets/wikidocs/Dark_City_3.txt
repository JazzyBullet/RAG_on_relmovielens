The movie titled 'Dark City' is directed by Alex Proyas and was released in 1998. The genre of this movie is Film-Noir|Sci-Fi|Thriller, with main cast including Rufus Sewell,William Hurt,Kiefer Sutherland,Jennifer Connelly,Richard O'Brien. It has a runtime of 100 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: A man struggles with memories of his past, including a wife he cannot remember, in a nightmarish world with no sun and run by beings with telekinetic powers who seek the souls of humans.
