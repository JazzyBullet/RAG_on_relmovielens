The movie titled 'Supernova' is directed by Walter Hill and was released in 2000. The genre of this movie is Adventure|Sci-Fi, with main cast including James Spader,Angela Bassett,Robert Forster,Lou Diamond Phillips,Peter Facinelli. It has a runtime of 91 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: Set in the 22nd century, when a battered salvage ship sends out a distress signal, the seasoned crew of the rescue hospital ship Nova-17 responds. What they find is a black hole--that threatens to destroy both ships--and a mysterious survivor whose body quickly mutates into a monstrous and deadly form.
