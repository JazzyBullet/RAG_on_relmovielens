The movie titled 'Remember the Titans' is directed by Boaz Yakin and was released in 2000.0. The plot summary is as follows: After leading his football team to 15 winning seasons, coach Bill Yoast is demoted and replaced by Herman Boone - tough, opinionated and as different from the beloved Yoast as he could be.
