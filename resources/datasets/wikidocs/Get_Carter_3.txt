The movie titled 'Get Carter' is directed by Stephen T. Kay and was released in 2000.0. The genre of this movie is Action|Drama|Thriller, with main cast including Sylvester Stallone,Miranda Richardson,Rachael Leigh Cook,Alan Cumming,Mickey Rourke. The plot summary is as follows: Jack Carter, a mob enforcer living in Las Vegas, travels back to his hometown of Seattle for his brother's funeral.
