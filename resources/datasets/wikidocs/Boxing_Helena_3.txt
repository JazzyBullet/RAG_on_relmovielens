The movie titled 'Boxing Helena' is directed by Jennifer Chambers Lynch and was released in 1993. The genre of this movie is Mystery|Romance|Thriller, with main cast including Julian Sands,Sherilyn Fenn,Bill Paxton,Art Garfunkel,Betsy Clark. It has a runtime of 107 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: A top surgeon is besotted with a beautiful woman who once rebuffed him. Unable to come to terms with life without her, he tries to convince her that they need each other. She has other ideas, but a horrific accident leaves her at his mercy.
