The movie titled 'Coogan's Bluff' is directed by Don Siegel and was released in 1968. The genre of this movie is Crime, with main cast including Clint Eastwood,Lee J. Cobb,Susan Clark,Tisha Sterling,Don Stroud. It has a runtime of 93 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Coogan, an Arizona deputy sheriff goes to New York to pick up a prisoner. While escorting the prisoner to the airport, he escapes and Coogan heads into the city to recapture him.
