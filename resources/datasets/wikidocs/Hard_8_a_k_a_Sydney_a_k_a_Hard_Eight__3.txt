The movie titled 'Hard 8 (a.k.a. Sydney, a.k.a. Hard Eight)' is directed by Paul Thomas Anderson and was released in 1997. The genre of this movie is Crime|Thriller, with main cast including Philip Baker Hall,John C. Reilly,Gwyneth Paltrow,Samuel L. Jackson,F. William Parker. It has a runtime of 102 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: A stranger mentors a young Reno gambler who weds a hooker and befriends a vulgar casino regular.
