The movie titled 'Drunken Master (Zui quan)' is directed by Yuen Woo-Ping and was released in 1978. The genre of this movie is Action|Comedy, with main cast including Jackie Chan,Yuen Siu-Tien,Hwang Jang-Lee,Lam Kau,Linda Lin Ying. It has a runtime of 111 minutes and languages used including 广州话 / 廣州話, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: After being punished for getting into trouble, a mischievous young man is sent to train under a brutal, but slovenly old beggar, who teaches him the secret of the Drunken Fist.
