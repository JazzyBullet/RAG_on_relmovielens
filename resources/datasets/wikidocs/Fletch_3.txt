The movie titled 'Fletch' is directed by Michael Ritchie and was released in 1985. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Chevy Chase,Tim Matheson,Dana Wheeler-Nicholson,Joe Don Baker,Richard Libertini. It has a runtime of 98 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: When investigative reporter Irwin "Fletch" Fletcher goes undercover to write a piece on the drug trade at a local beach, he's approached by wealthy businessman Alan Stanwyk, who offers him $50,000 to murder him. With sarcastic wit and a knack for disguises, Fletch sets out to uncover Stanwyk's story.
