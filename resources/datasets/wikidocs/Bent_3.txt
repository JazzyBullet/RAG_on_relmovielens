The movie titled 'Bent' is directed by Sean Mathias and was released in 1997. The genre of this movie is Drama|War, with main cast including Lothaire Bluteau,Clive Owen,Brian Webber,Ian McKellen,Mick Jagger. It has a runtime of 105 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: Max is a handsome young man who, after a fateful tryst with a German soldier, is forced to run for his life. Eventually Max is placed in a concentration camp where he pretends to be Jewish because in the eyes of the Nazis, gays are the lowest form of human being. But it takes a relationship with an openly gay prisoner to teach Max that without the love of another, life is not worth living.
