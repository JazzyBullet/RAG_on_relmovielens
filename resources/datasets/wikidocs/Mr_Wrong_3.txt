The movie titled 'Mr. Wrong' is directed by Nick Castle and was released in 1996. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Ellen DeGeneres,Bill Pullman,Joan Cusack,Dean Stockwell,Joan Plowright. It has a runtime of 96 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: A single and lonely woman finds the seemingly perfect man to date, but soon regrets it when his deranged and possessive other personality emerges and worst still, she cannot convince anyone else of his Jekyll/Hyde true nature.
