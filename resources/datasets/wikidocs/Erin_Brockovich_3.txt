The movie titled 'Erin Brockovich' is directed by Steven Soderbergh and was released in 2000.0. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Julia Roberts,Albert Finney,Aaron Eckhart,Marg Helgenberger,Cherry Jones. It has a runtime of 131 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: A twice-divorced mother of three who sees an injustice, takes on the bad guy and wins -- with a little help from her push-up bra. Erin goes to work for an attorney and comes across medical records describing illnesses clustered in one nearby town. She starts investigating and soon exposes a monumental cover-up.
