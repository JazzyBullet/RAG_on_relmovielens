The movie titled 'See the Sea (Regarde la mer)' is directed by François Ozon and was released in 1997. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Sasha Hails,Marina de Van,Samantha,Paul Raoux. It has a runtime of 52 minutes and languages used including English,Français, with a Certificate rating of NR.
