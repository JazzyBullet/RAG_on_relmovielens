The movie titled 'Clockers' is directed by Spike Lee and was released in 1995.0. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Harvey Keitel,John Turturro,Delroy Lindo,Mekhi Phifer,Isaiah Washington. It has a runtime of 128 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Strike is a young city drug pusher under the tutelage of drug lord Rodney Little. When a night manager at a fast-food restaurant is found with four bullets in his body, Strike's older brother turns himself in as the killer. Detective Rocco Klein doesn't buy the story, however, setting out to find the truth, and it seems that all the fingers point toward Strike & Rodney.
