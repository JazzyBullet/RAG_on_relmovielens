The movie titled 'Hunt for Red October, The' is directed by John McTiernan and was released in 1990.0. The genre of this movie is Action|Thriller, with main cast including Sean Connery,Alec Baldwin,Scott Glenn,Sam Neill,James Earl Jones. It has a runtime of 135 minutes and languages used including English,Pусский, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: A new technologically-superior Soviet nuclear sub, the Red October, is heading for the U.S. coast under the command of Captain Marko Ramius. The American government thinks Ramius is planning to attack. Lone CIA analyst Jack Ryan has a different idea: he thinks Ramius is planning to defect, but he has only a few hours to find him and prove it - because the entire Russian naval and air commands are trying to find Ramius, too. The hunt is on!
