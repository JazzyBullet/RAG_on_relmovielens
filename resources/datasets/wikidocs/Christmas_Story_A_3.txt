The movie titled 'Christmas Story, A' is directed by Bob Clark and was released in 1983. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Drama, with main cast including Peter Billingsley,Melinda Dillon,Darren McGavin,Jean Shepherd,Ian Petrella. It has a runtime of 93 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: The comic mishaps and adventures of a young boy named Ralph, trying to convince his parents, teachers, and Santa that a Red Ryder B.B. gun really is the perfect Christmas gift for the 1940s.
