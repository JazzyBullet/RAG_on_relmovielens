The movie titled 'Barney's Great Adventure' is directed by Steve Gomer and was released in 1998. The genre of this movie is Adventure|Children's, with main cast including Kyla Pratt,Trevor Morgan,George Hearn,Shirley Douglas,Alan Fawcett. It has a runtime of 76 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of G. The plot summary is as follows: Mom and dad dump son Cody, daughter Abby, her best friend Marcella and a baby on the farm with Grandpa and Grandma. Purple dinosaur Barney soon appears to entertain the kids, and when a large colorful egg deposited on a farm by a shooting star is accidentally carted off, Barney and the kids start their chase for it.
