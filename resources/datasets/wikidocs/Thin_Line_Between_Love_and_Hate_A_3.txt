The movie titled 'Thin Line Between Love and Hate, A' is directed by Martin Lawrence and was released in 1996. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Lynn Whitfield,Martin Lawrence,Regina King,Della Reese,Bobby Brown. It has a runtime of 108 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Nightclub manager Darnell Wright is a perpetual playboy who is almost as devoted to his job as he is to the pursuit of beautiful women. After he sets his sights on the ultra-classy Brandi Web, he launches an all-out assault to win her heart. Ultimately, charm, lust and passion prevail, but Darnell learns the hard way that when you play, you pay. Brandi is much harder to get rid of than she was to get--especially when she realizes that she has a rival vying for Darnell's affection. When he finally decides to call it quits, Brandi becomes an obsessed femme fatale stalking the new love of her life.
