The movie titled 'Airheads' is directed by Michael Lehmann and was released in 1994. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Brendan Fraser,Steve Buscemi,Adam Sandler,Joe Mantegna,Chris Farley. It has a runtime of 92 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: The Lone Rangers have heavy-metal dreams and a single demo tape they can't get anyone to play. The solution: Hijack an FM rock radio station and hold the deejays hostage until they agree to broadcast the band's tape.
