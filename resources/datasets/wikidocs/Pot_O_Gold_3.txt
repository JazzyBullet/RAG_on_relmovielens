The movie titled 'Pot O' Gold' is directed by George Marshall and was released in 1941. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Musical, with main cast including James Stewart,Paulette Goddard,Horace Heidt,Charles Winninger,Mary Gordon. It has a runtime of 86 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: Jimmy, the owner of a failed music shop, goes to work with his uncle, the owner of a food factory. Before he gets there, he befriends an Irish family who happens to be his uncle's worst enemy because of their love for music and in-house band who constantly practices. Soon, Jimmy finds himself trying to help the band by getting them gigs and trying to reconcile the family with his uncle.
