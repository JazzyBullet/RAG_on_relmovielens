The movie titled 'Nightmare on Elm Street 5: The Dream Child, A' is directed by Stephen Hopkins and was released in 1989. The genre of this movie is Horror, with main cast including Robert Englund,Lisa Wilcox,Erika Anderson,Valorie Armstrong,Michael Ashton. It has a runtime of 89 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Alice, having survived the previous installment of the Nightmare series, finds the deadly dreams of Freddy Krueger starting once again. This time, the taunting murderer is striking through the sleeping mind of Alice's unborn child. His intention is to be "born again" into the real world. The only one who can stop Freddy is his dead mother, but can Alice free her spirit in time to save her own son?
