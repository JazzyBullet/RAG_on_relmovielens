The movie titled 'Swiss Family Robinson' is directed by Ken Annakin and was released in 1960. The genre of this movie is Adventure|Children's, with main cast including John Mills,Dorothy McGuire,James MacArthur,Janet Munro,Sessue Hayakawa. It has a runtime of 126 minutes and languages used including English,Italiano, with a Certificate rating of G. The plot summary is as follows: After being shipwrecked, the Robinson family is marooned on an island inhabited only by an impressive array of wildlife. In true pioneer spirit, they quickly make themselves at home but soon face a danger even greater than nature: dastardly pirates.
