The movie titled 'Hillbillys in a Haunted House' is directed by Jean Yarbrough and was released in 1967. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Ferlin Husky,Joi Lansing,Don Bowman,John Carradine,Lon Chaney Jr.. It has a runtime of 86 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: Country singers on their way to Nashville have car trouble, forcing them to stop at an old haunted mansion. Soon they realize that the house is not only haunted, but is also the headquarters of a ring of international spies after a top secret formula for rocket fuel.
