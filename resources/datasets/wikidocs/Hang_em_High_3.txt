The movie titled 'Hang 'em High' is directed by Ted Post and was released in 1968. The genre of this movie is Western, with main cast including Clint Eastwood,Inger Stevens,Ed Begley,Pat Hingle,Ben Johnson. It has a runtime of 114 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: Marshall Jed Cooper survives a hanging, vowing revenge on the lynch mob that left him dangling. To carry out his oath for vengeance, he returns to his former job as a lawman. Before long, he's caught up with the nine men on his hit list and starts dispensing his own brand of Wild West justice.
