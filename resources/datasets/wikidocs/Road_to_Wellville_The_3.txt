The movie titled 'Road to Wellville, The' is directed by Alan Parker and was released in 1994. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Anthony Hopkins,Bridget Fonda,Matthew Broderick,John Cusack,Dana Carvey. It has a runtime of 118 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: An unhappy young couple visit the infamous Kellogg spa in Battle Creek, Michigan while a young hustler tries get into the breakfast-cereal business and compete against John Kellogg's corn flakes.
