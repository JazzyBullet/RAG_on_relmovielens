The movie titled 'Clay Pigeons' is directed by David Dobkin and was released in 1998. The genre of this movie is Crime, with main cast including Joaquin Phoenix,Vince Vaughn,Janeane Garofalo,Scott Wilson,Gregory Sporleder. It has a runtime of 104 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Clay is a young man in a small town who witnesses his friend, Earl kill himself because of the ongoing affair that Clay was having with the man's wife, Amanda. Feeling guilty, Clay now resists the widow when she presses him to continue with their sexual affairs. Clay inadvertently befriends a serial killer named Lester Long, who murders the widow in an attempt to "help" his "fishing buddy."
