The movie titled 'Trip to Bountiful, The' is directed by Peter Masterson and was released in 1985. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Geraldine Page,John Heard,Carlin Glynn,Richard Bradford,Rebecca De Mornay. It has a runtime of 108 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: Carrie Watts is living the twilight of her life trapped in an apartment in 1940s Houston, Texas with a controlling daughter-in-law and a hen-pecked son. Her fondest wish - just once before she dies - is to revisit Bountiful, the small Texas town of her youth which she still refers to as "home."
