The movie titled 'Fly Away Home' is directed by Carroll Ballard and was released in 1996. The genre of this movie is Adventure|Children's, with main cast including Jeff Daniels,Anna Paquin,Dana Delany,Terry Kinney,Holter Graham. It has a runtime of 107 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: Amy is only 13 years old when her mother is killed. She goes to Canada to live with her father, an eccentric inventor whom she barely knows. Amy is miserable in her new life... until she discovers a nest of goose eggs that were abandoned when a local forest was torn down. The eggs hatch and Amy becomes "Mama Goose". When Winter comes, Amy, and her dad must find a way to lead the birds South.
