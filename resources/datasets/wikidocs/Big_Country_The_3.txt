The movie titled 'Big Country, The' is directed by William Wyler and was released in 1958.0. The genre of this movie is Romance|Western, with main cast including Gregory Peck,Jean Simmons,Carroll Baker,Charlton Heston,Burl Ives. It has a runtime of 166 minutes and languages used including English,Español, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: Retired wealthy sea captain Jim McKay arrives in the vast expanse of the West to marry fiancée Pat Terrill. McKay is a man whose values and approach to life are a mystery to the ranchers and ranch foreman Steve Leech takes an immediate dislike to him. Pat is spoiled, selfish and controlled by her wealthy father, Major Henry Terrill. The Major is involved in a ruthless civil war, over watering rights for cattle, with a rough hewn clan led by Rufus Hannassey. The land in question is owned by Julie Maragon and both Terrill and Hannassey want it.
