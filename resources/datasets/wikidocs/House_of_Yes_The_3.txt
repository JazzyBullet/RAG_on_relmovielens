The movie titled 'House of Yes, The' is directed by Mark Waters and was released in 1997. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Drama|Thriller, with main cast including Parker Posey,Josh Hamilton,Tori Spelling,Freddie Prinze Jr.,Geneviève Bujold. It has a runtime of 85 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Jackie-O is anxiously awaiting the visit of her brother home for Thanksgiving, but isn't expecting him to bring a friend — and she's even more shocked to learn that this friend is his fiance. It soon becomes clear that her obsession with Jackie Kennedy is nothing compared to her obsession with her brother, and she isn't the only member of the family with problems.
