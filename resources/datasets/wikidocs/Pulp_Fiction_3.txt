The movie titled 'Pulp Fiction' is directed by Quentin Tarantino and was released in 1994. The genre of this movie is Crime|Drama, with main cast including John Travolta,Samuel L. Jackson,Uma Thurman,Bruce Willis,Ving Rhames. It has a runtime of 154 minutes and languages used including English,Español,Français, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: A burger-loving hit man, his philosophical partner, a drug-addled gangster's moll and a washed-up boxer converge in this sprawling, comedic crime caper. Their adventures unfurl in three stories that ingeniously trip back and forth in time.
