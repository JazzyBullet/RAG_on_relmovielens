The movie titled 'Tess of the Storm Country' is directed by John S. Robertson and was released in 1922. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Mary Pickford,Lloyd Hughes,Gloria Hope,David Torrence,Forrest Robinson. It has a runtime of 118 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: Wealthy Elias Graves builds his home on the top of a hill, where a group of squatters have taken up residence at the bottom. Many of the men in the squatters' village have their eyes on young Tess, and one of them, Ben Letts, frames Tess's father for murder. While maintaining her father's innocence, Tess must keep her love for Graves' son a secret, while caring for Elias' daughter's illegitamate child.
