The movie titled 'Day of the Beast, The (El día de la bestia)' is directed by Álex de la Iglesia and was released in 1995. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Horror|Thriller, with main cast including Álex Angulo,Armando De Razza,Santiago Segura,Terele Pávez,Nathalie Seseña. It has a runtime of 103 minutes and languages used including Italiano,Español, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: The story revolves around a Basque Roman Catholic priest dedicated to committing as many sins as possible, a death metal salesman from Carabanchel, and the Italian host of a TV show on the occult. These go on a literal "trip" through Christmas-time Madrid to hunt for and prevent the reincarnation of the Antichrist.
