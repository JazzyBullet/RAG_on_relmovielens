The movie titled 'Hangmen Also Die' is directed by Fritz Lang and was released in 1943.0. The genre of this movie is Drama|War, with main cast including Brian Donlevy,Walter Brennan,Anna Lee,Gene Lockhart,Dennis O'Keefe. It has a runtime of 134 minutes and languages used including English,Deutsch, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: During the Nazi occupation of Czechoslovakia, surgeon Dr. Franticek Svoboda, a Czech patriot, assassinates the brutal "Hangman of Europe", Reichsprotektor Reinhard Heydrich, and is wounded in the process. In his attempt to escape, he is helped by history professor Stephen Novotny and his daughter Mascha.
