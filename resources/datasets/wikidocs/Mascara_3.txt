The movie titled 'Mascara' is directed by Linda Kandel and was released in 1999. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Ione Skye,Lumi Cavazos,Amanda de Cadenet,Steven Schub,Steve Jones. It has a runtime of 94 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Mascara is the story of three very different women who rediscover the value of their friendship at a time when their lives are in turmoil. Panic sets in as they approach their thirtieth birthdays and nothing is going according to plan.
