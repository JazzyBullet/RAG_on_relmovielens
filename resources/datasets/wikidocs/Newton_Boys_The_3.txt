The movie titled 'Newton Boys, The' is directed by Richard Linklater and was released in 1998. The genre of this movie is Crime|Drama, with main cast including Matthew McConaughey,Skeet Ulrich,Ethan Hawke,Vincent D'Onofrio,Dwight Yoakam. It has a runtime of 122 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: The four Newton brothers are a poor farmer family in the 1920s. One day, the oldest of them, Willis, realizes that there's no future in the fields and offers his brothers to become bank robbers. Soon the family agrees. They become very famous robbers and execute the greatest train robbery in American history five years later.
