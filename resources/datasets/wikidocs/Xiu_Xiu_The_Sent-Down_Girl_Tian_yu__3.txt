The movie titled 'Xiu Xiu: The Sent-Down Girl (Tian yu)' is directed by Joan Chen and was released in 1998.0. The genre of this movie is Drama|Romance, with main cast including Li Xiaolu,Lopsang,Zheng Qian,Jie Gao,Qianqian Li. The plot summary is as follows: Young teen girl Xiu Xiu is sent away to a remote corner of the Sichuan steppes for manual labor in 1975 (sending young people to there was a part of Cultural Revolution in China). A year later, she agrees to go to even more remote spot with a Tibetan saddle tramp Lao Jin to learn horse herding.
