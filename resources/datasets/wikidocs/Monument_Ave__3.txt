The movie titled 'Monument Ave.' is directed by Ted Demme and was released in 1998. The genre of this movie is Crime, with main cast including Denis Leary,Ian Hart,Jason Barry,Lenny Clarke,Kevin Chapman. It has a runtime of 93 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: Bobby O'Grady is a low level member of a Boston Irish gang run by Jackie O'Hara. Jackie demands absolute, total loyalty to him. When Jackie kills one of Bobby's buddies, Teddy, Bobby and others have to keep it an absolute secret, even from their and Teddy's relatives.
