The movie titled 'Halloween' is directed by John Carpenter and was released in 1978. The genre of this movie is Horror, with main cast including Donald Pleasence,Jamie Lee Curtis,P.J. Soles,Nancy Kyes,Charles Cyphers. It has a runtime of 91 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Fifteen years after murdering his sister on Halloween Night 1963, Michael Myers escapes from a mental hospital and returns to the small town of Haddonfield, Illinois to kill again.
