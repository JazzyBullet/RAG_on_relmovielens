The movie titled 'Poison Ivy II' is directed by Anne Goursaud and was released in 1996. The genre of this movie is Thriller, with main cast including Alyssa Milano,Johnathon Schaech,Xander Berkeley,Belinda Bauer,Camilla Belle. It has a runtime of 106 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: A young and naive college art student becomes obsessed with assuming the identity and personality of a departed coed who used to live in her room, and in so doing causes complications that result in two men, a student and her art professor, lusting after her.
