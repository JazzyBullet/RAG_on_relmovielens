The movie titled 'Gods and Monsters' is directed by Bill Condon and was released in 1998. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Ian McKellen,Brendan Fraser,Lynn Redgrave,Lolita Davidovich,David Dukes. It has a runtime of 105 minutes and languages used including English,Magyar, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: It's 1957, and James Whale's heyday as the director of "Frankenstein," "Bride of Frankenstein" and "The Invisible Man" is long behind him. Retired and a semi-recluse, he lives his days accompanied only by images from his past. When his dour housekeeper, Hannah, hires a handsome young gardener, the flamboyant director and simple yard man develop an unlikely friendship, which will change them forever.
