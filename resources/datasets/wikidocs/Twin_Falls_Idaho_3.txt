The movie titled 'Twin Falls Idaho' is directed by Michael Polish and was released in 1999. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Mark Polish,Michael Polish,Michele Hicks,Jon Gries,Patrick Bauchau. It has a runtime of 111 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: Francis and Blake Falls are Siamese twins who live in a neat little room in a rundown hotel. While sharing some organs, Blake is always fit and Francis is very sickly. Into their world comes a young lady, who turns their world upside down. She gets involved with Blake, and convinces the two to attend a Halloween party, where they can pass themselves off as wearing a costume. Eventually Francis becomes really ill, and they have to be separated. They then face the physical and mental strains that come from their proposed separation.
