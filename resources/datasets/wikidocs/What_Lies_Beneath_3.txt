The movie titled 'What Lies Beneath' is directed by Robert Zemeckis and was released in 2000. The genre of this movie is Thriller, with main cast including Harrison Ford,Michelle Pfeiffer,Diana Scarwid,James Remar,Miranda Otto. It has a runtime of 130 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: When Claire Spencer starts hearing ghostly voices and seeing spooky images, she wonders if an otherworldly spirit is trying to contact her. All the while, her husband tries to reassure her by telling her it's all in her head. But as Claire investigates, she discovers that the man she loves might know more than he's letting on.
