The movie titled 'Young Guns' is directed by Christopher Cain and was released in 1988. The genre of this movie is Action|Comedy|Western, with main cast including Emilio Estevez,Kiefer Sutherland,Lou Diamond Phillips,Charlie Sheen,Dermot Mulroney. It has a runtime of 107 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: A group of young gunmen, led by Billy the Kid, become deputies to avenge the murder of the rancher who became their benefactor. But when Billy takes their authority too far, they become the hunted.
