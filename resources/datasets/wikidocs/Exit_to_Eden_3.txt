The movie titled 'Exit to Eden' is directed by Garry Marshall and was released in 1994. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Dana Delany,Paul Mercurio,Rosie O'Donnell,Dan Aykroyd,Hector Elizondo. It has a runtime of 114 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Photographer Elliot Slater has inadvertently snapped the only existing photos of elusive jewel thief Omar. When Elliot leaves for a private vacation on an S&M island, he is followed by Omar; Omar's partner, Nina; and police officers Fred and Sheila. Unaware of the number of people chasing him, Elliot enjoys his stay at the resort, and finds himself falling in love with head dominatrix Lisa.
