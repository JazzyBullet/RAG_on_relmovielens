The movie titled 'Vertigo' is directed by Alfred Hitchcock and was released in 1958. The genre of this movie is Mystery|Thriller, with main cast including James Stewart,Kim Novak,Barbara Bel Geddes,Tom Helmore,Henry Jones. It has a runtime of 128 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: A retired San Francisco detective suffering from acrophobia investigates the strange activities of an old friend's wife, all the while becoming dangerously obsessed with her.
