The movie titled 'Friday the 13th Part V: A New Beginning' is directed by Danny Steinmann and was released in 1985. The genre of this movie is Horror, with main cast including Tiffany Helm,John Shepherd,Juliette Cummins,Melanie Kinnaman,Richard Young. It has a runtime of 92 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Homicidal maniac Jason returns from the grave to cause more bloody mayhem. Young Tommy may have escaped from Crystal Lake, but he's still haunted by the gruesome events that happened there. When gory murders start happening at the secluded halfway house for troubled teens where he now lives, it seems like his nightmarish nemesis, Jason, is back for more sadistic slaughters.
