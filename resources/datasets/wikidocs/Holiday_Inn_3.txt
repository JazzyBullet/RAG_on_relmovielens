The movie titled 'Holiday Inn' is directed by Mark Sandrich and was released in 1942. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Musical, with main cast including Bing Crosby,Fred Astaire,Marjorie Reynolds,Virginia Dale,Walter Abel. It has a runtime of 101 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: Lovely Linda Mason has crooner Jim Hardy head over heels, but suave stepper Ted Hanover wants her for his new dance partner after fickle Lila Dixon gives him the brush. Jim's supper club, Holiday Inn, is the setting for the chase by Hanover and his manager.
