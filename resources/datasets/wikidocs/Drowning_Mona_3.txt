The movie titled 'Drowning Mona' is directed by Nick Gomez and was released in 2000.0. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Danny DeVito,Bette Midler,Neve Campbell,Jamie Lee Curtis,Casey Affleck. It has a runtime of 96 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: The recently deceased Mona Dearly was many things: an abusive wife, a domineering mother, a loud-mouthed neighbor and a violent malcontent. So when her car and corpse are discovered in the Hudson River, police Chief Wyatt Rash immediately suspects murder rather than an accident. But, since the whole community of Verplanck, N.Y., shares a deep hatred for this unceasingly spiteful woman, Rash finds his murder investigation overwhelmed with potential suspects.
