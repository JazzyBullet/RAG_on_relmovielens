The movie titled 'Mariachi, El' is directed by Robert Rodriguez and was released in 1992.0. The genre of this movie is Action|Thriller, with main cast including Carlos Gallardo,Consuelo Gómez,Jaime de Hoyos,Peter Marquardt,Reinol Martinez. It has a runtime of 81 minutes and languages used including English,Español, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: El Mariachi just wants to play his guitar and carry on the family tradition. Unfortunately, the town he tries to find work in has another visitor, a killer who carries his guns in a guitar case. The drug lord and his henchmen mistake el Mariachi for the killer, Azul, and chase him around town trying to kill him and get his guitar case.
