The movie titled 'Rain' is directed by Lewis Milestone and was released in 1932. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Joan Crawford,Walter Huston,Matt Moore,Guy Kibbee,William Gargan. It has a runtime of 94 minutes and languages used including ,English, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: Due to a possible cholera epidemic onboard, passengers on a ship are forced to disembark at Pago Pago, a small village on a Pacific island where it incessantly rains. Among the stranded passengers are Sadie Thompson, a prostitute, and Alfred Davidson, a fanatic missionary who will try to redeem her.
