Return to Paradise is a British-Australian crime drama TV series which is  set to air in 2024. It is the second spin-off series of long-running crime series Death in Paradise, and stars Home and Away actress Anna Samson as DI Mackenzie Clarke.


== Cast ==
The cast of the series were announced the same day as the announcement for the series was made, with Anna Samson announced as the lead. Former Wentworth star Celia Ireland was named to return to television after a multi-year absence from TV.

Anna Samson as DI Mackenzie Clarke
Lloyd Griffith
Tai Hara
Celia Ireland as Reggie Rocco
Catherine McClements
Andrea Dimitriades
Aaron McGrath
