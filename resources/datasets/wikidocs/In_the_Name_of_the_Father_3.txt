The movie titled 'In the Name of the Father' is directed by Jim Sheridan and was released in 1993. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Daniel Day-Lewis,Pete Postlethwaite,Emma Thompson,John Lynch,Corin Redgrave. It has a runtime of 133 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: A small time thief from Belfast, Gerry Conlon, is falsely implicated in the IRA bombing of a pub that kills several people while he is in London. He and his four friends are coerced by British police into confessing their guilt. Gerry's father and other relatives in London are also implicated in the crime. He spends fifteen years in prison with his father trying to prove his innocence.
