The movie titled 'Fly, The' is directed by David Cronenberg and was released in 1986. The genre of this movie is Horror|Sci-Fi, with main cast including Jeff Goldblum,Geena Davis,John Getz,Joy Boushel,Leslie Carlson. It has a runtime of 96 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: When Seth Brundle makes a huge scientific and technological breakthrough in teleportation, he decides to test it on himself. Unbeknownst to him, a common housefly manages to get inside the device and the two become one.
