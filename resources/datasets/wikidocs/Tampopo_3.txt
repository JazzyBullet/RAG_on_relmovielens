The movie titled 'Tampopo' is directed by Jūzō Itami and was released in 1985.0. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Tsutomu Yamazaki,Nobuko Miyamoto,Ken Watanabe,Koji Yakusho,Rikiya Yasuoka. It has a runtime of 115 minutes and languages used including 日本語, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: In this humorous paean to the joys of food, a pair of truck drivers happen onto a decrepit roadside shop selling ramen noodles. The widowed owner, Tampopo, begs them to help her turn her establishment into a paragon of the "art of noodle-soup making". Interspersed are satirical vignettes about the importance of food to different aspects of human life.
