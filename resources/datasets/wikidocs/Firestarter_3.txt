The movie titled 'Firestarter' is directed by Mark L. Lester and was released in 1984.0. The genre of this movie is Horror|Thriller, with main cast including Drew Barrymore,David Keith,Freddie Jones,Heather Locklear,Martin Sheen. It has a runtime of 114 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: As youths, Andy McGee and his future wife, Vicky, participated in secret experiments, allowing themselves to be subjected to mysterious medical tests. Years later, the couple's daughter, Charlie, begins to exhibit the ability of setting fires solely with her mind. This volatile talent makes the youngster extremely dangerous and soon she becomes a target for the enigmatic agency known as "The Shop."
