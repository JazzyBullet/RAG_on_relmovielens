The movie titled 'My Favorite Martian' is directed by Donald Petrie and was released in 1999.0. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Sci-Fi, with main cast including Jeff Daniels,Elizabeth Hurley,Daryl Hannah,Christopher Lloyd,Wallace Shawn. It has a runtime of 94 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: News producer, Tim O'Hara gets himself fired for unwillingly compromising his bosses' daughter during a live transmission. A little later, he witnesses the crashing of a small Martian spacecraft, realizing his one-time chance of delivering a story that will rock the earth. Since Tim took the original but scaled-down spaceship with him, the Martian follows him to retrieve it.
