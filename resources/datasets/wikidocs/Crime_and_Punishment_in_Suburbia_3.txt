The movie titled 'Crime and Punishment in Suburbia' is directed by Rob Schmidt and was released in 2000. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Drama, with main cast including Monica Keena,Ellen Barkin,Michael Ironside,Vincent Kartheiser,James DeBello. It has a runtime of 100 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: At school, Roseanne is the object of fellow student Vincent's infatuation. By night, she deals with a troubled family life: her mother, Maggie, cheats on her drunken husband, Fred. When Maggie's adultery is revealed, Fred viciously takes his anger out on stepdaughter Roseanne. With the help of her boyfriend, Jimmy, Maggie plots her revenge, but Vincent might be the one to help her forge a new life in this contemporary fable loosely based on Fyodor Dostoyevsky's "Crime and Punishment".
