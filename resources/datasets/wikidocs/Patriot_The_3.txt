The movie titled 'Patriot, The' is directed by Roland Emmerich and was released in 2000. The genre of this movie is Action|Drama|War, with main cast including Mel Gibson,Heath Ledger,Joely Richardson,Jason Isaacs,Chris Cooper. It has a runtime of 165 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: After proving himself on the field of battle in the French and Indian War, Benjamin Martin wants nothing more to do with such things, preferring the simple life of a farmer. But when his son Gabriel enlists in the army to defend their new nation, America, against the British, Benjamin reluctantly returns to his old life to protect his son.
