Prom Night IV: Deliver Us from Evil is a 1991 Canadian slasher film directed by Clay Borris and starring Nicole de Boer and J.H. Wyman. The film follows a deranged Catholic priest who targets four teenagers on their prom night. It is the fourth and final film in the original Prom Night film series. Like the previous film, it was released briefly in theaters before later being released to video.
