The movie titled 'Air America' is directed by Roger Spottiswoode and was released in 1990. The genre of this movie is Action|Comedy, with main cast including Mel Gibson,Robert Downey Jr.,Nancy Travis,Ken Jenkins,David Marshall Grant. It has a runtime of 112 minutes and languages used including English,, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Air America was the CIA's private airline operating in Laos during the Vietnam War, running anything and everything from soldiers to foodstuffs for local villagers. After losing his pilot's license, Billy Covington is recruited into it, and ends up in the middle of a bunch of lunatic pilots, gun-running by his friend Gene Ryack, and opium smuggling by his own superiors.
