The movie titled 'Fear and Loathing in Las Vegas' is directed by Terry Gilliam and was released in 1998.0. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Drama, with main cast including Johnny Depp,Benicio del Toro,Tobey Maguire,Michael Lee Gogin,Larry Cedar. It has a runtime of 118 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Raoul Duke and his attorney Dr. Gonzo drive a red convertible across the Mojave desert to Las Vegas with a suitcase full of drugs to cover a motorcycle race. As their consumption of drugs increases at an alarming rate, the stoned duo trash their hotel room and fear legal repercussions. Duke begins to drive back to L.A., but after an odd run-in with a cop, he returns to Sin City and continues his wild drug binge.
