The movie titled 'Beverly Hills Ninja' is directed by Dennis Dugan and was released in 1997. The genre of this movie is Action|Comedy, with main cast including Chris Farley,Nicollette Sheridan,Robin Shou,Nathaniel Parker,Chris Rock. It has a runtime of 88 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: Haru, an orphaned American who washes ashore in Japan and is mistaken for the great White Ninja of legend. Raised among the finest Ninjas, Haru grows strong and big - very big. With the grace of all Three Stooges rolled into one body, Haru is an embarrassment to his clan. But when a beautiful blonde pleads for his help, Haru is given one dangerous, disastrously funny chance to prove himself.
