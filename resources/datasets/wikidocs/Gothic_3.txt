The movie titled 'Gothic' is directed by Ken Russell and was released in 1986. The genre of this movie is Drama|Horror, with main cast including Gabriel Byrne,Julian Sands,Natasha Richardson,Myriam Cyr,Timothy Spall. It has a runtime of 84 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Living on an estate on the shores of Lake Geneva, Lord Byron is visited by Percy and Mary Shelley. Together with Byron's lover Claire Clairmont, and aided by hallucinogenic substances, they devise an evening of ghoulish tales. However, when confronted by horrors, ostensibly of their own creation, it becomes difficult to tell apparition from reality.
