The movie titled 'Clean Slate' is directed by Mick Jackson and was released in 1994. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Dana Carvey,Valeria Golino,James Earl Jones,Kevin Pollak,Michael Gambon. It has a runtime of 107 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: Pogue is a private eye with a problem: every morning when he wakes up, he has total amnesia, waking up with a 'blank slate'. Since he is in the middle of a hot investigation and has a developing romance, this is less than convenient.
