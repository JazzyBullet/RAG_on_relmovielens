The movie titled 'Tom and Huck' is directed by Peter Hewitt and was released in 1995. The genre of this movie is Adventure|Children's, with main cast including Jonathan Taylor Thomas,Brad Renfro,Eric Schweig,Charles Rocket,Amy Wright. It has a runtime of 97 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: A mischievous young boy, Tom Sawyer, witnesses a murder by the deadly Injun Joe. Tom becomes friends with Huckleberry Finn, a boy with no future and no family. Tom has to choose between honoring a friendship or honoring an oath because the town alcoholic is accused of the murder. Tom and Huck go through several adventures trying to retrieve evidence.
