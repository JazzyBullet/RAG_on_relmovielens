The movie titled 'All That Jazz' is directed by Bob Fosse and was released in 1979. The genre of this movie is Musical, with main cast including Roy Scheider,Ann Reinking,Leland Palmer,Jessica Lange,Erzsebet Foldi. It has a runtime of 123 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Joe Gideon is at the top of the heap, one of the most successful directors and choreographers in musical theater. But he can feel his world slowly collapsing around him - his obsession with work has almost destroyed his personal life, and only his bottles of pills keep him going.
