The movie titled 'Some Like It Hot' is directed by Billy Wilder and was released in 1959. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Crime, with main cast including Tony Curtis,Jack Lemmon,Marilyn Monroe,George Raft,Pat O'Brien. It has a runtime of 122 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: Two musicians witness a mob hit and struggle to find a way out of the city before they are found by the gangsters. Their only opportunity is to join an all-girl band as they leave on a tour. To make their getaway they must first disguise themselves as women, then keep their identities secret and deal with the problems this brings - such as an attractive bandmate and a very determined suitor.
