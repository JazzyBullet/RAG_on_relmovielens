The movie titled 'Double Life of Veronique, The (La Double Vie de Véronique)' is directed by Krzysztof Kieślowski and was released in 1991.0. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Irène Jacob,Halina Gryglaszewska,Philippe Volter,Guillaume de Tonquédec,Kalina Jędrusik. It has a runtime of 98 minutes and languages used including Français,Italiano,Polski, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Véronique is a beautiful young French woman who aspires to be a renowned singer; Weronika lives in Poland, has a similar career goal and looks identical to Véronique, though the two are not related. The film follows both women as they contend with the ups and downs of their individual lives, with Véronique embarking on an unusual romance with Alexandre Fabbri, a puppeteer who may be able to help her with her existential issues.
