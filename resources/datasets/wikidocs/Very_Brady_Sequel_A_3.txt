The movie titled 'Very Brady Sequel, A' is directed by Arlene Sanford and was released in 1996. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Shelley Long,Gary Cole,Christopher Daniel Barnes,Christine Taylor,Paul Sutera. It has a runtime of 90 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: A man claiming to be Carol Brady's long-lost first husband, Roy Martin, shows up at the suburban Brady residence one evening. An impostor, the man is actually determined to steal the Bradys' familiar horse statue, a $20-million ancient Asian artifact.
