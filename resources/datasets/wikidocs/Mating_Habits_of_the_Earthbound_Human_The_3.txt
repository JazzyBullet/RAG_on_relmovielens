The movie titled 'Mating Habits of the Earthbound Human, The' is directed by Jeff Abugov and was released in 1999. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Mackenzie Astin,Carmen Electra,David Hyde Pierce,Markus Redmond,Lucy Liu. It has a runtime of 90 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: David Hyde Pierce, playing an alien (credited as infinity-cubed in the opening credits), narrates a courtship in a late-20th century American city as an extraterrestrial nature documentary. The relationship "footage" is played straight, while the voice-over (with its most often wildly inaccurate theories) and elaborate visual metaphors add comedy.
