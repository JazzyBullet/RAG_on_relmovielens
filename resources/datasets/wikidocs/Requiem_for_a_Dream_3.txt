The movie titled 'Requiem for a Dream' is directed by Darren Aronofsky and was released in 2000. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Ellen Burstyn,Jared Leto,Jennifer Connelly,Marlon Wayans,Christopher McDonald. It has a runtime of 102 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of NC-17. The plot summary is as follows: The hopes and dreams of four ambitious people are shattered when their drug addictions begin spiraling out of control. A look into addiction and how it overcomes the mind and body.
