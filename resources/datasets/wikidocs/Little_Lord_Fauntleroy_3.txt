The movie titled 'Little Lord Fauntleroy' is directed by John Cromwell and was released in 1936. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Freddie Bartholomew,Dolores Costello,C. Aubrey Smith,Guy Kibbee,Henry Stephenson. It has a runtime of 102 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: An American boy turns out to be the heir of a wealthy British earl. He is sent to live with the irritable and unsentimental aristocrat, his grandfather.
