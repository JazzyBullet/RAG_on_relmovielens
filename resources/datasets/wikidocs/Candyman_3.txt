The movie titled 'Candyman' is directed by Bernard Rose and was released in 1992. The genre of this movie is Horror, with main cast including Virginia Madsen,Tony Todd,Xander Berkeley,Kasi Lemmons,Vanessa Williams. It has a runtime of 100 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: The Candyman, a murderous soul with a hook for a hand, is accidentally summoned to reality by a skeptic grad student researching the monster's myth.
