The movie titled 'Top Gun' is directed by Tony Scott and was released in 1986. The genre of this movie is Action|Romance, with main cast including Tom Cruise,Kelly McGillis,Val Kilmer,Anthony Edwards,Tom Skerritt. It has a runtime of 110 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: For Lieutenant Pete 'Maverick' Mitchell and his friend and co-pilot Nick 'Goose' Bradshaw, being accepted into an elite training school for fighter pilots is a dream come true. But a tragedy, as well as personal demons, will threaten Pete's dreams of becoming an ace pilot.
