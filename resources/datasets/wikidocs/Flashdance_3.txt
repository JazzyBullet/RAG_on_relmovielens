The movie titled 'Flashdance' is directed by Adrian Lyne and was released in 1983. The genre of this movie is Drama|Romance, with main cast including Jennifer Beals,Michael Nouri,Sunny Johnson,Kyle T. Heffner,Cynthia Rhodes. It has a runtime of 95 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Alex Owens, a teen juggling between two odd jobs, aspires to become a successful ballet dancer. Nick, who is her boss and lover, supports and encourages her to fulfil her dream.
