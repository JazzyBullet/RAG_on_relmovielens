The movie titled 'Zeus and Roxanne' is directed by George T. Miller and was released in 1997. The genre of this movie is Children's, with main cast including Steve Guttenberg,Kathleen Quinlan,Arnold Vosloo,Dawn McMillan,Jessica Howell. It has a runtime of 98 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: Mary Beth is a marine biologist that gets annoyed when a dog called Zeus stows aboard her research boat. Nevertheless she is intrigued when the intrusive canine makes best-friends with her captive dolphin, Roxanne. She falls in love with Zeus's owner, Terry, a musician who rides a bike.
