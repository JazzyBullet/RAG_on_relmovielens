The movie titled 'Children of the Corn II: The Final Sacrifice' is directed by David Price and was released in 1992. The genre of this movie is Horror, with main cast including Terence Knox,Paul Scherrer,Ryan Bollman,Christie Clark,Rosalind Allen. It has a runtime of 92 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: When a tabloid reporter and his son travel to a quiet Midwestern town to investigate a gruesome massacre, they fall victim to a possessed orphan named Micah.
