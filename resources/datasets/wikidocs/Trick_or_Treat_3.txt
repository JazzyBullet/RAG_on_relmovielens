The plot summary is as follows: Eddie Weinbauer, a metalhead teen who is bullied at school, looks to his heavy metal superstar idol, Sammi Curr, for guidance. As Halloween approaches, Eddie begins to realize that this isn't only rock 'n roll...it's life and death.
