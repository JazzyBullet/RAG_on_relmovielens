The movie titled 'Ran' is directed by Akira Kurosawa and was released in 1985. The genre of this movie is Drama|War, with main cast including Tatsuya Nakadai,Akira Terao,Jinpachi Nezu,Daisuke Ryū,Mieko Harada. It has a runtime of 160 minutes and languages used including 日本語, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: With Ran, legendary director Akira Kurosawa reimagines Shakespeare's King Lear as a singular historical epic set in sixteenth-century Japan. Majestic in scope, the film is Kurosawa's late-life masterpiece, a profound examination of the folly of war and the crumbling of one family under the weight of betrayal, greed, and the insatiable thirst for power.
