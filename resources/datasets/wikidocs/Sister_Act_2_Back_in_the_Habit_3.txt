The movie titled 'Sister Act 2: Back in the Habit' is directed by Bill Duke and was released in 1993. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Whoopi Goldberg,Kathy Najimy,Lauryn Hill,Sheryl Lee Ralph,Maggie Smith. It has a runtime of 107 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: Deloris Van Cartier is again asked to don the nun's habit to help a run-down Catholic school, presided over by Mother Superior. And if trying to reach out to a class full of uninterested students wasn't bad enough, the sisters discover that the school is due to be closed by the unscrupulous chief of a local authority.
