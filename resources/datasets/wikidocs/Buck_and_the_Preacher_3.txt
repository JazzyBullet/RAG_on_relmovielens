The movie titled 'Buck and the Preacher' is directed by Sidney Poitier and was released in 1972. The genre of this movie is Western, with main cast including Sidney Poitier,Harry Belafonte,Ruby Dee,Cameron Mitchell,Denny Miller. It has a runtime of 102 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: A wagon master and a con-man preacher help freed slaves dogged by cheap-labor agents out West.
