The movie titled 'Psycho II' is directed by Richard Franklin and was released in 1983. The genre of this movie is Horror|Thriller, with main cast including Anthony Perkins,Vera Miles,Meg Tilly,Robert Loggia,Dennis Franz. It has a runtime of 113 minutes and languages used including English,Italiano, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: After twenty-two years of psychiatric care, Norman Bates attempts to return to a life of solitude, but the specters of his crimes, and his long-deceased mother, continue to haunt him.
