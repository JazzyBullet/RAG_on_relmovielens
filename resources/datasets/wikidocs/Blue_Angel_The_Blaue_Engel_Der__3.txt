The movie titled 'Blue Angel, The (Blaue Engel, Der)' is directed by Josef von Sternberg and was released in 1930.0. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Emil Jannings,Marlene Dietrich,Kurt Gerron,Rosa Valetti,Hans Albers. It has a runtime of 108 minutes and languages used including Deutsch, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: Prim professor Immanuel Rath finds some of his students ogling racy photos of cabaret performer Lola Lola and visits a local club, The Blue Angel, in an attempt to catch them there. Seeing Lola perform, the teacher is filled with lust, eventually resigning his position at the school to marry the young woman. However, his marriage to a coquette -- whose job is to entice men -- proves to be more difficult than Rath imagined.
