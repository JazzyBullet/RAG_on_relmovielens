The movie titled 'Cleo From 5 to 7 (Cléo de 5 à 7)' is directed by Agnès Varda and was released in 1962.0. The plot summary is as follows: Agnès Varda eloquently captures Paris in the sixties with this real-time portrait of a singer set adrift in the city as she awaits test results of a biopsy. A chronicle of the minutes of one woman's life, Cléo from 5 to 7 is a spirited mix of vivid vérité and melodrama, featuring a score by Michel Legrand and cameos by Jean-Luc Godard and Anna Karina.
