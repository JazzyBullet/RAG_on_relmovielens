The movie titled 'Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles III' is directed by Stuart Gillard and was released in 1993. The genre of this movie is Action|Children's|Fantasy, with main cast including Elias Koteas,Paige Turco,Stuart Wilson,Sab Shimono,Vivian Wu. It has a runtime of 96 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: The four turtles travel back in time to the days of the legendary and deadly samurai in ancient Japan, where they train to perfect the art of becoming one. The turtles also assist a small village in an uprising.
