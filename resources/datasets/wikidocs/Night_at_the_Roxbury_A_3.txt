The movie titled 'Night at the Roxbury, A' is directed by John Fortenberry and was released in 1998. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Chris Kattan,Will Ferrell,Dan Hedaya,Molly Shannon,Richard Grieco. It has a runtime of 81 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: Despite being well into adulthood, brothers Doug and Steve Butabi still live at home and work in the flower shop owned by their dad. They exist only to hit on women at discos, though they're routinely unsuccessful until a chance run-in with Richard Grieco gets them inside the swank Roxbury club. Mistaken for high rollers, they meet their dream women, Vivica and Cambi, and resolve to open a club of their own.
