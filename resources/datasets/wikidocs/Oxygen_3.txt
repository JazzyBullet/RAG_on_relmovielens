The movie titled 'Oxygen' is directed by Richard Shepard and was released in 1999.0. The genre of this movie is Thriller, with main cast including Maura Tierney,Adrien Brody,James Naughton,Laila Robins,Terry Kinney. It has a runtime of 92 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: When housewife Frances Hannon is abducted and buried alive, detective Madeline Foster is brought in. With only 24 hours before Frances' oxygen runs out, Madeline pursues the trail laid by a killer calling himself Harry Houdini. After capturing him, Madeline brings Harry back to the police station, but is unable to get him to confess where Frances is buried. As time runs down, Harry gets inside the head of unstable, alcoholic Madeline.
