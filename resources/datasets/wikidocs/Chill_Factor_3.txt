The movie titled 'Chill Factor' is directed by Hugh Johnson and was released in 1999.0. The genre of this movie is Action|Thriller, with main cast including Cuba Gooding Jr.,Skeet Ulrich,Peter Firth,David Paymer,Hudson Leick. It has a runtime of 101 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: A store clerk and an ice cream truck driver are thrown together when a dying scientist entrusts them with a deadly chemical kept in ice. This chemical will kill every living thing once it melts. They have to take the chemical codenamed 'Elvis' to the next nearest military base while being chased by terrorists who want it to hold the country for ransom.
