The movie titled 'Easy Rider' is directed by Dennis Hopper and was released in 1969. The genre of this movie is Adventure|Drama, with main cast including Peter Fonda,Dennis Hopper,Jack Nicholson,Antonio Mendoza,Phil Spector. It has a runtime of 95 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R.
