The movie titled 'Poltergeist III' is directed by Gary Sherman and was released in 1988. The genre of this movie is Horror|Thriller, with main cast including Tom Skerritt,Nancy Allen,Heather O'Rourke,Lara Flynn Boyle,Kipley Wentz. It has a runtime of 98 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13.
