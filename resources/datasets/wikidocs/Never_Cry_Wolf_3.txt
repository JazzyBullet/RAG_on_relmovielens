The movie titled 'Never Cry Wolf' is directed by Carroll Ballard and was released in 1983. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Charles Martin Smith,Zachary Ittimangnaq,Samson Jorah,Hugh Webster,Brian Dennehy. It has a runtime of 105 minutes and languages used including English,, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: A scientific researcher, sent on a government study: The Lupus Project, must investigate the possible "menace" of wolves in the north. To do so, he must survive in the wilderness for six months on his own. In the course of these events, he learns about the true beneficial and positive nature of the wolf species. Based on the book and true story by Farley Mowat.
