The movie titled 'Waltzes from Vienna' is directed by Alfred Hitchcock and was released in 1934. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Musical, with main cast including Edmund Gwenn,Esmond Knight,Jessie Matthews,Fay Compton,Frank Vosper.
