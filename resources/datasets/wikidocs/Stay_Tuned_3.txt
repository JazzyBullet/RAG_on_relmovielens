The movie titled 'Stay Tuned' is directed by Peter Hyams and was released in 1992. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including John Ritter,Pam Dawber,Jeffrey Jones,David Tom,Heather McComb. It has a runtime of 87 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: Salesman Roy Knable spends all his free time watching television, to the exasperation of his wife, Helen. One day, TV salesman Spike convinces Roy to buy a satellite dish offering 666 channels. The new addition to Roy's home entertainment system sucks him and Helen into Hellvision, a realm run by Spike, who is an emissary of Satan. For 24 hours, the couple must survive devilish parodies of TV programs if they want to return to reality alive.
