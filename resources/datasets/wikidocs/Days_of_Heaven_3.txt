The movie titled 'Days of Heaven' is directed by Terrence Malick and was released in 1978. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Richard Gere,Brooke Adams,Sam Shepard,Linda Manz,Robert J. Wilke. It has a runtime of 94 minutes and languages used including English,Italiano, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: In 1916, a Chicago steel worker accidentally kills his supervisor and flees to the Texas panhandle with his girlfriend and little sister to work harvesting wheat in the fields of a stoic farmer.
