The movie titled 'Needful Things' is directed by Fraser Clarke Heston and was released in 1993. The genre of this movie is Drama|Horror, with main cast including Max von Sydow,Ed Harris,Bonnie Bedelia,Amanda Plummer,J.T. Walsh. It has a runtime of 120 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: A mysterious new shop opens in a small town which always seems to stock the deepest desires of each shopper, with a price far heavier than expected.
