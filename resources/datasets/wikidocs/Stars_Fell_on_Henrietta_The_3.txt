The movie titled 'Stars Fell on Henrietta, The' is directed by James Keach and was released in 1995. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Robert Duvall,Aidan Quinn,Frances Fisher,Brian Dennehy,Lexi Randall. It has a runtime of 109 minutes and languages used including Magyar,English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: The setting is early America during the oil boom. An elderly, down on his luck 'oil man', Mr. Cox finds himself in the town of Henrietta. Using unconventional methods, he convinces himself and local Don Day that there is oil on Day's land. The financially strapped Day puts everything into finding oil...but at what cost?
