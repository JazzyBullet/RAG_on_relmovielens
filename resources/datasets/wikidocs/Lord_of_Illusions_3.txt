The movie titled 'Lord of Illusions' is directed by Clive Barker and was released in 1995. The genre of this movie is Horror, with main cast including Scott Bakula,Kevin J. O'Connor,Famke Janssen,Joel Swetow,Daniel von Bargen. It has a runtime of 119 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: During a routine case in L.A., NY private investigator Harry D'Amour stumbles over members of a fanatic cult who are preparing for the resurrection of their leader Nix, a powerful magician who was killed 13 years earlier.
