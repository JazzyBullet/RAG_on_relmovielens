The movie titled 'Operation Condor 2 (Longxiong hudi)' is directed by Jackie Chan,Eric Tsang and was released in 1986. The genre of this movie is Action|Adventure|Comedy, with main cast including Jackie Chan,Alan Tam,Rosamund Kwan,Lola Forner,Božidar Smiljanić. It has a runtime of 98 minutes and languages used including 广州话 / 廣州話, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: Jackie Chan stars as Asian Hawk, an Indiana Jones-style adventurer looking to make a fortune in exotic antiquities. After Hawk discovers a mysterious sword in Africa, a band of Satan-worshipping monks kidnap his ex-girlfriend Lorelei, demanding the sword as ransom as well as other pieces of the legendary Armour of God - a magical outfit dating back to the Crusades.
