The movie titled 'Fearless' is directed by Peter Weir and was released in 1993. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Jeff Bridges,Isabella Rossellini,Rosie Perez,Tom Hulce,John Turturro. It has a runtime of 122 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: After a terrible air disaster, survivor Max Klein emerges a changed person. Unable to connect to his former life or to wife Laura, he feels godlike and invulnerable. When psychologist Bill Perlman is unable to help Max, he has Max meet another survivor, Carla Rodrigo, who is wracked with grief and guilt since her baby died in the crash which she and Max survived.
