The movie titled 'Great Day in Harlem, A' is directed by Jean Bach and was released in 1994. The genre of this movie is Documentary, with main cast including Quincy Jones,Dizzy Gillespie,Sonny Rollins,Buck Clayton. It has a runtime of 60 minutes and languages used including nan, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: Art Kane, now deceased, coordinated a group photograph of all the top jazz musicians in NYC in the year 1958, for a piece in Esquire magazine. Just about every jazz musician at the time showed up for the photo shoot which took place in front of a brownstone near the 125th street station. The documentary compiles interviews of many of the musicians in the photograph to talk about the day of the photograph, and it shows film footage taken that day by Milt Hinton and his wife.
