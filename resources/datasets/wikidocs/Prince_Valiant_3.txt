The movie titled 'Prince Valiant' is directed by Anthony Hickox and was released in 1997. The genre of this movie is Adventure, with main cast including Stephen Moyer,Katherine Heigl,Thomas Kretschmann,Edward Fox,Udo Kier. It has a runtime of 91 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: An Arthurian legend of young Prince Valiant, son of the King of Scandia. After the King is exiled by an evil leader, the Prince travels to Camelot to secure the aid of King Arthur in helping restore his family to power and prevent a plot by the Black Knight.
