The movie titled 'Liar Liar' is directed by Tom Shadyac and was released in 1997. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Jim Carrey,Maura Tierney,Justin Cooper,Cary Elwes,Anne Haney. It has a runtime of 86 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: Fletcher Reede is a fast-talking attorney and habitual liar. When his son Max blows out the candles on his fifth birthday he has just one wish - that his dad will stop lying for 24 hours. When Max's wish comes true, Fletcher discovers that his mouth has suddenly become his biggest liability.
