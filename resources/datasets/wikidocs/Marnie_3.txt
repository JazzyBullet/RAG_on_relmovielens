The movie titled 'Marnie' is directed by Alfred Hitchcock and was released in 1964. The genre of this movie is Thriller, with main cast including Tippi Hedren,Sean Connery,Diane Baker,Martin Gabel,Louise Latham. It has a runtime of 130 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: Marnie is a thief, a liar, and a cheat. When her new boss, Mark Rutland, catches on to her routine kleptomania, she finds herself being blackmailed.
