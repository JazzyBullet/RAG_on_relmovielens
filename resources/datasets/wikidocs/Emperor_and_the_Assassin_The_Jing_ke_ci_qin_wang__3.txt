The movie titled 'Emperor and the Assassin, The (Jing ke ci qin wang)' is directed by Chen Kaige and was released in 1998. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Gong Li,Zhang Fengyi,Li Xuejian,Zhiwen Wang,Sun Zhou. It has a runtime of 162 minutes and languages used including 普通话,Polski, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: In pre-unified China, the King of Qin sends his concubine to a rival kingdom to produce an assassin for a political plot, but as the king's cruelty mounts she finds her loyalty faltering.
