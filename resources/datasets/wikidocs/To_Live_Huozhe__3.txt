The movie titled 'To Live (Huozhe)' is directed by Zhang Yimou and was released in 1994. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Ge You,Gong Li,Ben Niu,Jiang Wu,Guo Tao. It has a runtime of 133 minutes and languages used including 普通话, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: Fugui and Jiazhen endure tumultuous events in China as their personal fortunes move from wealthy landownership to peasantry. Addicted to gambling, Fugui loses everything. In the years that follow he is pressed into both the nationalist and communist armies, while Jiazhen is forced into menial work.
