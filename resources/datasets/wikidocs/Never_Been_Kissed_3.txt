The movie titled 'Never Been Kissed' is directed by Raja Gosnell and was released in 1999. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Romance, with main cast including Drew Barrymore,David Arquette,Molly Shannon,Michael Vartan,Jessica Alba. It has a runtime of 107 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: Josie Geller, a baby-faced junior copywriter at the Chicago Sun-Times, must pose as a student at her former high school to research contemporary teenage culture. With the help of her brother, Rob, Josie infiltrates the inner circle of the most popular clique on campus. But she hits a major snag in her investigation -- not to mention her own failed love life -- when she falls for her dreamy English teacher, Sam Coulson.
