The movie titled 'Gossip' is directed by Davis Guggenheim and was released in 2000. The genre of this movie is Drama|Thriller, with main cast including James Marsden,Lena Headey,Norman Reedus,Kate Hudson,Eric Bogosian. It has a runtime of 90 minutes and languages used including Italiano,English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: For a class project, three college students decide to invent an unfounded rumor about the most popular girl on campus. But as the rumor spreads, it begins to spiral out of control.
