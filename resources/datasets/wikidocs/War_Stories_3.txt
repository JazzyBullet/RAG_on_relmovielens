The movie titled 'War Stories' is directed by Gaylene Preston and was released in 1996. The genre of this movie is Documentary, with main cast including Jean Andrews,Judith Fyfe,Rita Graham,Neva Clarke McKenna,Tui Preston. It has a runtime of 95 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: Seven New Zealand women speak about their lives during World War II: some lost husbands, some got married, some went into service themselves.
