The movie titled 'Far and Away' is directed by Ron Howard and was released in 1992.0. The genre of this movie is Drama|Romance, with main cast including Tom Cruise,Nicole Kidman,Thomas Gibson,Robert Prosky,Barbara Babcock. It has a runtime of 140 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: A young man leaves Ireland with his landlord's daughter after some trouble with her father, and they dream of owning land at the big giveaway in Oklahoma ca. 1893. When they get to the new land, they find jobs and begin saving money. The man becomes a local barehands boxer, and rides in glory until he is beaten, then his employers steal all the couple's money and they must fight off starvation in the winter, and try to keep their dream of owning land alive. Meanwhile, the woman's parents find out where she has gone and have come to America to find her and take her back.
