The movie titled 'Snake Eyes' is directed by Brian De Palma and was released in 1998. The genre of this movie is Action|Crime|Mystery|Thriller, with main cast including Nicolas Cage,Gary Sinise,Carla Gugino,John Heard,Stan Shaw. It has a runtime of 98 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: All bets are off when shady homicide cop Rick Santoro witnesses a murder during a boxing match. It's up to him and lifelong friend, Naval intelligence agent Kevin Dunne to uncover the conspiracy behind the killing. At every turn, Santoro makes increasingly shocking discoveries that even he can't turn a blind eye to.
