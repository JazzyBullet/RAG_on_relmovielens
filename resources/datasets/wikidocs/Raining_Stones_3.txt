The movie titled 'Raining Stones' is directed by Ken Loach and was released in 1993. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Bruce Jones,Julie Brown,Gemma Phoenix,Ricky Tomlinson,Tom Hickey. It has a runtime of 90 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: Proud, though poor, Bob wants his little girl to have a beautiful (and costly) brand-new dress for her First Communion. His stubbornness and determination get him into trouble as he turns to more and more questionable measures, in his desperation to raise the needed money. This tragic flaw leads him to risk all that he loves and values, his beloved family, indeed even his immortal soul and salvation, in blind pursuit of that goal.
