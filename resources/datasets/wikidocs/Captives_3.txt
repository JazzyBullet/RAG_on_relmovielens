The movie titled 'Captives' is directed by Angela Pope and was released in 1994. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Julia Ormond,Tim Roth,Colin Salmon,Keith Allen,Mark Strong. It has a runtime of 100 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: A beautiful young dentist working in a tough British prison starts to become attracted to a violent inmate after the break-up of her marriage, and embarks upon an illicit affair with him, with terrible consequences for all.
