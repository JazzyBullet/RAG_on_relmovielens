The movie titled 'Rescuers Down Under, The' is directed by Mike Gabriel,Hendel Butoy and was released in 1990. The genre of this movie is Animation|Children's, with main cast including Bob Newhart,Eva Gabor,John Candy,Tristan Rogers,Adam Ryen. It has a runtime of 77 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of G. The plot summary is as follows: A lawless poacher wants to capture a majestic and rare golden eagle, so he kidnaps the boy who knows where to find the bird. Not to worry -- the Rescue Aid Society's top agents, heroic mice Miss Bianca and Bernard, fly to Australia to save the day. Accompanying the fearless duo are bumbling albatross Wilbur and local field operative Jake the Kangaroo Rat.
