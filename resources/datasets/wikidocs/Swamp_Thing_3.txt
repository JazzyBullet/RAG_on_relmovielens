The movie titled 'Swamp Thing' is directed by Wes Craven and was released in 1982. The genre of this movie is Horror|Sci-Fi, with main cast including Louis Jourdan,Adrienne Barbeau,Ray Wise,David Hess,Nicholas Worth. It has a runtime of 91 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: Dr. Alec Holland, hidden away in the depths of a murky swamp, is trying to create a new species - a combination of animal and plant capable of adapting and thriving in the harshest conditions. Unfortunately he becomes subject of his own creation and is transformed. Arcane, desperate for the formula, attempts to capture the Swamp Thing. An explosive chase ensues that ultimately ends with a confrontation between Holland and a changed Arcane...
