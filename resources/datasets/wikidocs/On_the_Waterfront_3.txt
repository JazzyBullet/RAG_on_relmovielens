The movie titled 'On the Waterfront' is directed by Elia Kazan and was released in 1954.0. The genre of this movie is Crime|Drama, with main cast including Marlon Brando,Karl Malden,Lee J. Cobb,Rod Steiger,Pat Henning. It has a runtime of 108 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: A dim-witted yet kind-hearted boxer, Terry Malloy, who failed to succeed unintentionally lures a man to his death after being tricked by a criminal called Johnny Friendly whose men pick of every man who has the courage to speak up to their crimes. As he works on the waterfronts that Friendly owns, he is sent to a church meeting run by a good preacher about how to deal with the problem and runs into the dead man's sister. Slowly, he falls in love with her and begins to feel guilt about his crime.
