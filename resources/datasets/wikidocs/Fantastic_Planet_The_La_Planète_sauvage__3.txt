The movie titled 'Fantastic Planet, The (La Planète sauvage)' is directed by René Laloux and was released in 1973. The genre of this movie is Animation|Sci-Fi, with main cast including Gérard Hernandez,Jean Valmont,Jennifer Drake,Yves Barsacq,Jeanine Forney. It has a runtime of 72 minutes and languages used including Français, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: On the planet Ygam, the Draags, extremely technologically and spiritually advanced blue humanoids, consider the tiny Oms, human beings descendants of Terra's inhabitants, as ignorant animals. Those who live in slavery are treated as simple pets and used to entertain Draag children; those who live hidden in the hostile wilderness of the planet are periodically hunted and ruthlessly slaughtered as if they were vermin.
