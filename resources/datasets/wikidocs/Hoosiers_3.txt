The movie titled 'Hoosiers' is directed by David Anspaugh and was released in 1986.0. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Gene Hackman,Barbara Hershey,Dennis Hopper,Sheb Wooley,Fern Persons. It has a runtime of 114 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: Failed college coach Norman Dale gets a chance at redemption when he is hired to coach a high school basketball team in a tiny Indiana town. After a teacher persuades star player Jimmy Chitwood to quit and focus on his long-neglected studies, Dale struggles to develop a winning team in the face of community criticism for his temper and his unconventional choice of assistant coach: Shooter, a notorious alcoholic.
