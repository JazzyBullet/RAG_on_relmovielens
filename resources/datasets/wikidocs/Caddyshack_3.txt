The movie titled 'Caddyshack' is directed by Harold Ramis and was released in 1980. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Chevy Chase,Rodney Dangerfield,Ted Knight,Bill Murray,Michael O'Keefe. It has a runtime of 98 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: At an exclusive country club, an ambitious young caddy, Danny Noonan, eagerly pursues a caddy scholarship in hopes of attending college and, in turn, avoiding a job at the lumber yard. In order to succeed, he must first win the favour of the elitist Judge Smails, and then the caddy golf tournament which Smails sponsors.
