The movie titled 'Awakenings' is directed by Penny Marshall and was released in 1990.0. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Robert De Niro,Robin Williams,John Heard,Julie Kavner,Penelope Ann Miller. It has a runtime of 120 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: Dr. Malcolm Sayer, a shy research physician, uses an experimental drug to "awaken" the catatonic victims of a rare disease. Leonard is the first patient to receive the controversial treatment. His awakening, filled with awe and enthusiasm, proves a rebirth for Sayer too, as the exuberant patient reveals life's simple but unutterably sweet pleasures to the introverted doctor.
