The movie titled 'Belle de jour' is directed by Luis Buñuel and was released in 1967. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Catherine Deneuve,Jean Sorel,Michel Piccoli,Geneviève Page,Pierre Clémenti. It has a runtime of 101 minutes and languages used including Français,,Español, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Beautiful young housewife Séverine Serizy cannot reconcile her masochistic fantasies with her everyday life alongside dutiful husband Pierre. When her lovestruck friend Henri mentions a secretive high-class brothel run by Madame Anais, Séverine begins to work there during the day under the name Belle de Jour. But when one of her clients grows possessive, she must try to go back to her normal life.
