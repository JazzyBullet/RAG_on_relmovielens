The movie titled 'Mildred Pierce' is directed by Michael Curtiz and was released in 1945. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Joan Crawford,Jack Carson,Zachary Scott,Eve Arden,Ann Blyth. It has a runtime of 111 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: A hard-working mother inches towards disaster as she divorces her husband and starts a successful restaurant business to support her spoiled daughter.
