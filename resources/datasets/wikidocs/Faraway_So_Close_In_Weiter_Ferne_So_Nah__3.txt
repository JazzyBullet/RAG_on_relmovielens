The movie titled 'Faraway, So Close (In Weiter Ferne, So Nah!)' is directed by Wim Wenders and was released in 1993. The genre of this movie is Drama|Fantasy, with main cast including Otto Sander,Bruno Ganz,Nastassja Kinski,Peter Falk,Solveig Dommartin. It has a runtime of 146 minutes and languages used including English,Français,Deutsch,Italiano,Pусский, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: Damiel is now married to Marion, runs the pizzeria “Da Angelo” and the two have a child. The solitarily remaining angel Cassiel is more and more dissatisfied with his destiny as a mere observer of human life and finally decides to take the great leap. As Karl Engel he soon gets into a dubious milieu and finds himself as the assistant of the German American Baker, who makes his money with shady arms deals and sends films east in exchange for weapons. Cassiel's adventure turns into a “thriller” when he decides to put a stop to Baker's game.
