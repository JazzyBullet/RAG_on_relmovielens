The movie titled 'Touki Bouki (Journey of the Hyena)' is directed by Djibril Diop Mambéty and was released in 1973. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Magaye Niang,Myriam Niang,Christoph Colomb,Mustapha Ture,Aminata Fall. It has a runtime of 91 minutes and languages used including العربية,Français,Wolof, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: A cowherd with a skull-mounted motorcycle and a university student meet in Dakar; put off by life in Senegal, they plan to make money in Paris.  Screened at the Cannes Film Festival in 1973, Mambéty's film tells the story of Mory (Magaye Niang), a cow herder and Anta (Myriam Niang), a student, attempting to break free from their situation of circumstance. Steadily raising petty cash in order to save to go to Paris and leave their old lives behind, the film is a pertinent study of contemporary Senegal, which boasts an important narrative as well as a revolutionary approach to filmmaking.
