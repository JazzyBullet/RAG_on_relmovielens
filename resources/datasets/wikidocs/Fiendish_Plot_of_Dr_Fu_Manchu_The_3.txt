The movie titled 'Fiendish Plot of Dr. Fu Manchu, The' is directed by Piers Haggard and was released in 1980. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Peter Sellers,Helen Mirren,David Tomlinson,Sid Caesar,Simon Williams. It has a runtime of 108 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: Fu Manchu's 168th birthday celebration is dampened when a hapless flunky spills Fu's age-regressing elixir vitae. Fu sends his lackeys to round up ingredients for a new batch of elixir, starting with the Star of Leningrad diamond, nabbed from a Soviet exhibition in Washington. The FBI sends agents Capone and Williams to England to confer with Nayland Smith, an expert on Fu.
