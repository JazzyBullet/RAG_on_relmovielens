The movie titled 'Vanya on 42nd Street' is directed by Louis Malle and was released in 1994. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Wallace Shawn,Julianne Moore,Larry Pine,Brooke Smith,George Gaynes. It has a runtime of 120 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: An uninterrupted rehearsal of Chekhov's 1899 play "Uncle Vanya" played out by a company of actors. The setting is their run down theater with an unusable stage and crumbling ceiling. The play is shown act by act with the briefest of breaks to move props or for refreshments. The lack of costumes, real props and scenery is soon forgotten.
