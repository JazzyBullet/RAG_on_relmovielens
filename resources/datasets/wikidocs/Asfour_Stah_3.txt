The movie titled 'Asfour Stah' is directed by Férid Boughedir and was released in 1990. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Selim Boughedir,Mustafa Adouani,Rabia Ben Abdallah,Mohammed Driss,Hélène Catzaras. It has a runtime of 98 minutes and languages used including العربية, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: Twelve-year-old Noura dangles uncertainly in that difficult netherworld between childhood and adulthood. His growing libido has gotten him banned from the women's baths, where his mother took him when he was younger, but he's not yet old enough to participate in grown-up discussions with the men of his Tunisian village. Noura's only real friend is a troublemaker named Salih -- the village political outcast.
