The movie titled 'Mortal Kombat: Annihilation' is directed by John R. Leonetti and was released in 1997. The genre of this movie is Action|Adventure, with main cast including Robin Shou,Talisa Soto,James Remar,Sandra Hess,Lynn 'Red' Williams. It has a runtime of 95 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: A group of heroic warriors has only six days to save the planet in "Mortal Kombat Annihilation." To succeed they must survive the most spectacular series of challenges any human, or god, has ever encountered as they battle an evil warlord bent on taking control of Earth.
