The movie titled 'Rocketship X-M' is directed by Kurt Neumann and was released in 1950.0. The genre of this movie is Sci-Fi, with main cast including Lloyd Bridges,Osa Massen,John Emery,Noah Beery Jr.,Hugh O'Brian. It has a runtime of 77 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: Astronauts blast off to explore the moon on Rocketship X-M or "Rocketship eXploration Moon". A spacecraft malfunction and some fuel miscalculations cause them to end up landing on Mars. On Mars, evidence of a once powerful civilization is found. The scientists determined that an atomic war destroyed most of the Martians. Those that survived reverted to a caveman like existence.
