The movie titled 'Suburbans, The' is directed by Donal Lardner Ward and was released in 1999. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Craig Bierko,Amy Brenneman,Antonio Fargas,Will Ferrell,Tony Guma. It has a runtime of 87 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: An 80s one-hit wonder band named The Suburbans reform for a special performance at one of the ex-member's wedding. At the wedding, a young record company talent scout happens to be in the audience and decides to give the now 40-ish performers a comeback push. The film attempts to take a satirical look at the music business of the 90s and compare it to the simpler 80s scene.
