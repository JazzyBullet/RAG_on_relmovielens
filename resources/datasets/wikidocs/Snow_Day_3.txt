The movie titled 'Snow Day' is directed by Chris Koch and was released in 2000.0. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Mark Webber,Schuyler Fisk,Zena Grey,Chris Elliott,Chevy Chase. It has a runtime of 89 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: When an entire town in upstate New York is closed down by an unexpected snowfall, a "snow day" begins when a group of elementary school kids, led by Natalie Brandston, try to ensure that the schools stay closed by stopping a mechanical snowplow driver by trying to hijack his plow truck. Meanwhile, Natalie's big brother Hal is using this day to try to win the affections of Claire Bonner, the most popular girl in his high school, while Hal and Natalie's father Tom, a TV meteorologist, faces off against a rival meteorologist for weather coverage of the day's events.
