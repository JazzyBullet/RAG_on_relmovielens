The movie titled 'Children of Heaven, The (Bacheha-Ye Aseman)' is directed by Majid Majidi and was released in 1997. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Reza Naji,Amir Farrokh Hashemian,Bahare Seddiqi,Nafise Jafar-Mohammadi,Fereshte Sarabandi. It has a runtime of 89 minutes and languages used including فارسی, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: Zohre's shoes are gone; her older brother Ali lost them. They are poor, there are no shoes for Zohre until they come up with an idea: they will share one pair of shoes. School awaits.
