The movie titled 'Jefferson in Paris' is directed by James Ivory and was released in 1995.0. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Nick Nolte,Greta Scacchi,Thandiwe Newton,Gwyneth Paltrow,Jean-Pierre Aumont. The plot summary is as follows: His wife having recently died, Thomas Jefferson accepts the post of United States ambassador to pre-revolutionary France, though he finds it difficult to adjust to life in a country where the aristocracy subjugates an increasingly restless peasantry. In Paris, he becomes smitten with cultured artist Maria Cosway, but, when his daughter visits from Virginia accompanied by her attractive slave, Sally Hemings, Jefferson's attentions are diverted.
