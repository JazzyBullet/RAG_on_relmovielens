The movie titled 'Up Close and Personal' is directed by Jon Avnet and was released in 1996. The genre of this movie is Drama|Romance, with main cast including Robert Redford,Michelle Pfeiffer,Stockard Channing,Joe Mantegna,Kate Nelligan. It has a runtime of 119 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: Tally Atwater has a dream: to be a prime-time network newscaster. She pursues this dream with nothing but ambition, raw talent and a homemade demo tape. Warren Justice is a brilliant, hard edged, veteran newsman. He sees Tally has talent and becomes her mentor. Tally's career takes a meteoric rise and she and Warren fall in love. The romance that results is as intense and revealing as television news itself. Yet, each breaking story, every videotaped crisis that brings them together, also threatens to drive them apart...
