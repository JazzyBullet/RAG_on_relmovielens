The movie titled 'East Palace West Palace (Dong gong xi gong)' is directed by Zhang Yuan and was released in 1996. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Si Han,Hu Jun,Liu Yuxiao,Zhao Wei,Wang Quan. It has a runtime of 90 minutes and languages used including 普通话, with a Certificate rating of nan.
