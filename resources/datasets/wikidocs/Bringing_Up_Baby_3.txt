The movie titled 'Bringing Up Baby' is directed by Howard Hawks and was released in 1938. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Katharine Hepburn,Cary Grant,Charles Ruggles,Walter Catlett,Barry Fitzgerald. It has a runtime of 102 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: David Huxley is waiting to get a bone he needs for his museum collection. Through a series of strange circumstances, he meets Susan Vance, and the duo have a series of misadventures which include a leopard called Baby.
