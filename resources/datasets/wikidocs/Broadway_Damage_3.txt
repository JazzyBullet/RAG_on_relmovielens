The movie titled 'Broadway Damage' is directed by Victor Mignatti and was released in 1997. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Hugh Panaro,Michael Shawn Lucas,Mara Hobel,Aaron Williams,Alan Filderman. It has a runtime of 111 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: A quirky, romantic comedy about the complexity and frustration of finding a true love in the gay community. Marc is a struggling actor who finds an apartment in NYC by searching the obituaries. He lives with his out-of-work, eccentric fag-hag Cynthia. His best friend, Robert, is secretly in love with Marc, while Marc falls head over heels for flighty studio musician David. Things heat up as Marc's desire for David keeps him waiting by the phone, while Cynthia gets financially cut off from her rich parents and Robert struggles to express his feelings for Marc.
