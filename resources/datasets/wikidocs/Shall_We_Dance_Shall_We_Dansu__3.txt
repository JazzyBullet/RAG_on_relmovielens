The movie titled 'Shall We Dance? (Shall We Dansu?)' is directed by Masayuki Suô and was released in 1996.0. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Koji Yakusho,Tamiyo Kusakari,Naoto Takenaka,Eri Watanabe,Akira Emoto. It has a runtime of 136 minutes and languages used including English,日本語, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: A bored Japanese accountant sees a beautiful woman in the window of a ballroom dance studio. He secretly starts taking dancing lessons to be near her, and then over time discovers how much he loves ballroom dancing. His wife, meanwhile, has hired a private detective to find out why he has started coming home late smelling of perfume.
