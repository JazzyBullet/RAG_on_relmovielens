The movie titled 'Just Cause' is directed by Arne Glimcher and was released in 1995. The genre of this movie is Mystery|Thriller, with main cast including Sean Connery,Laurence Fishburne,Blair Underwood,Kate Capshaw,Ruby Dee. It has a runtime of 102 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: A Harvard professor is lured back into the courtroom after twenty-five years to take the case of a young black man condemned to death for the horrific murder of a child.
