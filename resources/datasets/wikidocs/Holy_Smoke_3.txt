The movie titled 'Holy Smoke' is directed by Jane Campion and was released in 1999. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Kate Winslet,Harvey Keitel,Julie Hamilton,Sophie Lee,Dan Wyllie. It has a runtime of 115 minutes and languages used including English,हिन्दी, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: While on a journey of discovery in exotic India, beautiful young Ruth Barron falls under the influence of a charismatic religious guru. Her desperate parents then hire PJ Waters, a macho cult de-programmer who confronts Ruth in a remote desert hideaway. But PJ quickly learns that he's met his match in the sexy, intelligent and iron-willed Ruth.
