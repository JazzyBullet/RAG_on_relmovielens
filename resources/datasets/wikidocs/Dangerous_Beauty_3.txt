The movie titled 'Dangerous Beauty' is directed by Marshall Herskovitz and was released in 1998.0. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Catherine McCormack,Rufus Sewell,Oliver Platt,Fred Ward,Naomi Watts. It has a runtime of 111 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: Veronica is brilliant, gifted and beautiful, but the handsome aristocrat she loves, Marco Venier, cannot marry her because she is penniless and of questionable family. So Veronica's mother, Paola, teaches her to become a courtesan, one of the exotic companions favored by the richest and most powerful Venetian men. Veronica courageously uses her charms to change destiny -- and to give herself a chance at true love.
