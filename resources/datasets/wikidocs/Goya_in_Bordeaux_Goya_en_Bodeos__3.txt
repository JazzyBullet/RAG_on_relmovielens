The movie titled 'Goya in Bordeaux (Goya en Bodeos)' is directed by Carlos Saura and was released in 1999. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Francisco Rabal,Jose Coronado,Dafne Fernández,Maribel Verdú,Eulalia Ramón. It has a runtime of 100 minutes and languages used including Français,Español, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: Francisco Goya (1746-1828), deaf and ill, lives the last years of his life in voluntary exile in Bordeaux, a Liberal protesting the oppressive rule of Ferdinand VII. He's living with his much younger wife Leocadia and their daughter Rosario. He continues to paint at night, and in flashbacks stirred by conversations with his daughter, by awful headaches, and by the befuddlement of age, he relives key times in his life.
