The movie titled 'Welcome to Woop-Woop' is directed by Stephan Elliott and was released in 1998. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Johnathon Schaech,Rod Taylor,Susie Porter,Richard Moir,Barry Humphries. It has a runtime of 106 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R.
