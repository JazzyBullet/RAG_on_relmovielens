The movie titled 'Running Man, The' is directed by Paul Michael Glaser and was released in 1987. The genre of this movie is Action|Adventure|Sci-Fi, with main cast including Arnold Schwarzenegger,Richard Dawson,María Conchita Alonso,Yaphet Kotto,Jim Brown. It has a runtime of 101 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: By 2017, the global economy has collapsed and U.S. society has become a totalitarian police state, censoring all cultural activity. The government pacifies the populace by broadcasting a number of game shows in which convicted criminals fight for their lives, including the gladiator-style The Running Man, hosted by the ruthless Damon Killian, where “runners” attempt to evade “stalkers” and certain death for a chance to be pardoned and set free.
