The movie titled 'Star Wars: Episode V - The Empire Strikes Back' is directed by Irvin Kershner and was released in 1980. The genre of this movie is Action|Adventure|Drama|Sci-Fi|War, with main cast including Mark Hamill,Harrison Ford,Carrie Fisher,Billy Dee Williams,Anthony Daniels. It has a runtime of 124 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: The epic saga continues as Luke Skywalker, in hopes of defeating the evil Galactic Empire, learns the ways of the Jedi from aging master Yoda. But Darth Vader is more determined than ever to capture Luke. Meanwhile, rebel leader Princess Leia, cocky Han Solo, Chewbacca, and droids C-3PO and R2-D2 are thrown into various stages of capture, betrayal and despair.
