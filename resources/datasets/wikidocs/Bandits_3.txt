The movie titled 'Bandits' is directed by Katja von Garnier and was released in 1997. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Katja Riemann,Jasmin Tabatabai,Nicolette Krebitz,Jutta Hoffmann,Hannes Jaenicke.
