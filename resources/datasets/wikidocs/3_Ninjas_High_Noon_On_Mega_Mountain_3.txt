The movie titled '3 Ninjas: High Noon On Mega Mountain' is directed by Sean McNamara and was released in 1998. The genre of this movie is Action|Children's, with main cast including Victor Wong,Mathew Botuchis,Hulk Hogan,Loni Anderson,Jim Varney. It has a runtime of 93 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: Three young boys, Rocky, Colt and Tum Tum together with their neighbor girl, computer whiz Amanda are visiting Mega Mountain amusement park when it is invaded by an army of ninjas led by evil Medusa, who wants to take over the park and hold the owners for ransom. Kids and retired TV star Dave Dragon, who made his farewell appearance at the park at the time the ninjas appeared, have to break Medusa's vicious plans.
