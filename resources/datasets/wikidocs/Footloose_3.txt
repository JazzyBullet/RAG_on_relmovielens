The movie titled 'Footloose' is directed by Herbert Ross and was released in 1984. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Kevin Bacon,Lori Singer,John Lithgow,Dianne Wiest,Chris Penn. It has a runtime of 107 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG. The plot summary is as follows: When teenager Ren and his family move from big-city Chicago to a small town in the West, he's in for a real case of culture shock after discovering he's living in a place where music and dancing are illegal.
