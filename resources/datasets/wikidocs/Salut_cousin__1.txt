Hi Cousin! (French: Salut cousin!) is a 1996 Algerian comedy film directed by Merzak Allouache. The film was selected as the Algerian entry for the Best Foreign Language Film at the 69th Academy Awards, but was not accepted as a nominee.


== Cast ==
Gad Elmaleh as Alilo
Messaoud Hattau as Mok (as Mess Hattou)
Magaly Berdy as Fatoumata
Ann-Gisel Glass as Laurence
Jean Benguigui as Maurice
Xavier Maly as Claude
