Grandview, U.S.A. is a 1984 American comedy-drama film directed by Randal Kleiser, and starring Jamie Lee Curtis, Patrick Swayze, C. Thomas Howell, Jennifer Jason Leigh, and Troy Donahue.
The original music score is composed by Thomas Newman. It was filmed on location in Pontiac and Fairbury, Illinois.
After being out of print on VHS for many years, the film released on DVD on October 4, 2011.
