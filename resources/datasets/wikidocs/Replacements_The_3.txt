The movie titled 'Replacements, The' is directed by Howard Deutch and was released in 2000.0. The genre of this movie is Comedy, with main cast including Keanu Reeves,Gene Hackman,Brooke Langton,Orlando Jones,Faizon Love. It has a runtime of 118 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: Maverick old-guard coach Jimmy McGinty is hired in the wake of a players' strike to help the Washington Sentinels advance to the playoffs. But that impossible dream hinges on whether his replacements can hunker down and do the job. So, McGinty dusts off his secret dossier of ex-players who never got a chance (or screwed up the one they were given) and knits together a bad-dream team of guys who just may give the Sentinels their title shot.
