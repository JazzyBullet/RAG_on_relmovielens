The movie titled 'Anna Karenina' is directed by Bernard Rose and was released in 1997. The genre of this movie is Drama|Romance, with main cast including Sophie Marceau,Sean Bean,Alfred Molina,Mia Kirshner,James Fox. It has a runtime of 108 minutes and languages used including English,Pусский, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: Anna Karenina, the wife of a Russian imperial minister, creates a high-society scandal by an affair with Count Vronsky, a dashing cavalry officer in 19th-century St. Petersburg.
