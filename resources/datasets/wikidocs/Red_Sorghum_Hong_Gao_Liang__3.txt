The genre of this movie is Drama|War, with main cast including Gong Li,Jiang Wen,Teng Ru-jun,Ji Liu,Ming Qian. It has a runtime of 91 minutes and languages used including 普通话,日本語, with a Certificate rating of nan. The plot summary is as follows: An old leper who owned a remote sorghum winery dies. Jiu'er, the wife bought by the leper, and her lover, identified only as "my Grandpa" by the narrator, take over the winery and set up an idealized quasi-matriarchal community headed by Jiu'er. When the Japanese invaders subject the area to their rule and cut down the sorghum to make way for a road, the community rises up and resists as the sorghum grows anew.
