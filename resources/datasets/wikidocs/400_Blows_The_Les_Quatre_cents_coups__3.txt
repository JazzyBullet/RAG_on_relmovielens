The movie titled '400 Blows, The (Les Quatre cents coups)' is directed by François Truffaut and was released in 1959. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Jean-Pierre Léaud,Claire Maurier,Albert Rémy,Georges Flamant,Patrick Auffay. It has a runtime of 99 minutes and languages used including English,Français, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: For young Parisian boy Antoine Doinel, life is one difficult situation after another. Surrounded by inconsiderate adults, including his neglectful parents, Antoine spends his days with his best friend, Rene, trying to plan for a better life. When one of their schemes goes awry, Antoine ends up in trouble with the law, leading to even more conflicts with unsympathetic authority figures.
