The movie titled 'Postman Always Rings Twice, The' is directed by Bob Rafelson and was released in 1981. The genre of this movie is Crime|Thriller, with main cast including Jack Nicholson,Jessica Lange,John Colicos,Michael Lerner,John P. Ryan. It has a runtime of 122 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: The sensuous wife of a lunch wagon proprietor and a rootless drifter begin a sordidly steamy affair and conspire to murder her Greek husband.
