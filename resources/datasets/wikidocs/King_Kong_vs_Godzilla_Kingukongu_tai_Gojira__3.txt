The movie titled 'King Kong vs. Godzilla (Kingukongu tai Gojira)' is directed by Ishirō Honda and was released in 1962. The genre of this movie is Action|Sci-Fi, with main cast including Tadao Takashima,Kenji Sahara,Yū Fujiki,Ichirō Arishima,Jun Tazaki. It has a runtime of 97 minutes and languages used including 日本語,English, with a Certificate rating of NR. The plot summary is as follows: The advertising director of Pacific Pharmaceuticals, frustrated with the low ratings of their sponsored TV program, seeks a more sensationalist approach. He orders his staff to Faro Island to capture King Kong for exploitation. As Godzilla re-emerges, a media frenzy generates with Pacific looking to capitalize off of the ultimate battle.
