The movie titled 'Johnny 100 Pesos' is directed by Gustavo Graef-Marino and was released in 1993. The genre of this movie is Action|Drama, with main cast including Armando Araiza,Patricia Rivera,Willy Semler,Aldo Parodi,Rodolfo Bravo. It has a runtime of 90 minutes and languages used including Español, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: Based on real events, 17-year-old Johnny García becomes involved with four older thugs who are planning to rob a money-laundering business masquerading as a video store. As they try to escape, a swarm of exploitative media attention awaits.
