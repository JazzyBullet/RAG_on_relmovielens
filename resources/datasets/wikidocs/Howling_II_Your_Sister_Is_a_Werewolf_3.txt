The movie titled 'Howling II: Your Sister Is a Werewolf' is directed by Philippe Mora and was released in 1985. The genre of this movie is Horror, with main cast including Christopher Lee,Annie McEnroe,Reb Brown,Marsha Hunt,Sybil Danning. It has a runtime of 91 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of R. The plot summary is as follows: After his sister is turned into a werewolf and subsequently killed, Ben White decides to help the enigmatic Stefan Crosscoe fight the growing population of lupine monsters, along with the lovely Jenny Templeton. Traveling to Transylvania, Ben, Jenny and Crosscoe attempt to hunt down the powerful werewolf queen, Stirba, and must face her furry followers, as well as other supernatural forces.
