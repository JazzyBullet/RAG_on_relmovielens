The movie titled 'I Dreamed of Africa' is directed by Hugh Hudson and was released in 2000.0. The genre of this movie is Drama, with main cast including Kim Basinger,Vincent Perez,Liam Aiken,Daniel Craig,Eva Marie Saint. It has a runtime of 114 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: Inspired by the true story of indomitable Kuki Gallmann, the film tells of a beautiful and inquisitive woman who had the courage to escape from her comfortable yet monotonous life in Italy to start anew in the African wilderness with her son, Emanuele, and her new husband, Paolo. Gallmann faces great danger there but eventually becomes a celebrated conservationist.
