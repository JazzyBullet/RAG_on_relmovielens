The movie titled 'Drop Dead Fred' is directed by Ate de Jong and was released in 1991. The genre of this movie is Comedy|Fantasy, with main cast including Phoebe Cates,Rik Mayall,Marsha Mason,Tim Matheson,Carrie Fisher. It has a runtime of 103 minutes and languages used including English, with a Certificate rating of PG-13. The plot summary is as follows: When Elizabeth returns to her mother's home after her marriage breaks up, she recreates her imaginary childhood friend, Fred, to escape from the trauma of losing her husband and her job. In between the chaos and mayhem that Fred creates, Elizabeth attempts to win back her husband and return to normality.
