The Threesome is an upcoming American romantic comedy film directed by Chad Hartigan, written by Ethan Ogilby and starring Zoey Deutch, Jonah Hauer-King and Ruby Cruz.

== Production ==
Directed by Chad Hartigan and scripted by Ethan Ogilby in his feature film writing debut having previously worked on long running television series The Simpsons. In 2022 the project had Logan Lerman and Phoebe Dynevor attached to lead roles. The film is produced by Tim White and Trevor White for Star Thrower Entertainment, Vince Jolivette for Filmopoly, and Steven Shapiro for Jupiter Peak. 
Principal photography took place in Arkansas in early 2024 with the working title Threeâ€™s a Crowd. Filming was completed in February 2024 with Zoey Deutch, Jonah Hauer-King and Ruby Cruz in the lead roles. On 21 February 2024, Arden Myrin was confirmed in the cast.
